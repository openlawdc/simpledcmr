§ 16-1400. PURPOSE AND SCOPE (§ 16-1400)

1400.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish administrative rules and procedures for the implementation of the Automobile Consumer Protection Act of 1984 (Act).

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is § 10 of the Automobile Consumer Protection Act of 1984, D.C. Law 5-162, D.C. Code § 50-508 (2001).

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 2958 (May 16, 1986).

EDITOR’S NOTE

Section 811 of D.C. Law 11-52 amended the Automobile Consumer Protection Act of 1984 by suspending enforcement of the act by the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs until October 1, 1998 (D.C. Official Code §50-501 et seq. (2001)).
