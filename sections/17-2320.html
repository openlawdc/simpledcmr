§ 17-2320. APPRAISAL INSTRUCTORS (§ 17-2320)

2320.1 2320.1 To be approved by the Board for prelicensing credit, a course instructorhall be an individual who has been approved as an instructor by one (1) of theona fide professional appraisal organizations that is a member of the Appraisaloundation, or be a professor or instructor at a college or university which hasn accredited department of real estate and economics.

2320.2 2320.2 An appraiser instructor of a prelicensing (residential appraisal) coursehall possess one hundred five (105) classroom hours of real estate appraisalducation and two thousand (2,000) hours experience as a residential real estateppraiser within the previous five (5) years.

2320.3 2320.3 An appraiser instructor of a precertification (general appraisal) coursehall possess two hundred five (205) classroom hours of real estate appraisalducation and two thousand (2,000) hours experience as a general real estateppraiser within the previous five (5) years, with at least one-half (1/2) ofuch experience being in income property appraising.

2320.4 2320.4 All USPAP courses taught for prelicensure or continuing educationurposes shall be taught by AQB-certified instructors who are also certifiedppraisers.
