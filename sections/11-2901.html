§ 11-2901. APPLICABILITY (§ 11-2901)

2901.1. The USN District is mapped on the following squares and air rights lots, as more specifically described in the plat attached to Zoning Commission Order No. 09-21:

(a) Square 717, Lots 7001 and 7002 (between H and K Streets, N.E.); and

(b) Square 720, Lots 7000 and 7001 (between Union Station and H Street, N.E.).

2901.2. The land and the volume of space below the air rights lots shall not be zoned USN. Development will occur on top of a structural platform that will span the railroad tracks underneath.

2901.3. The USN District shall not be applied to any lot located on the ground or any air rights lot outside of the boundaries described in § 2901.1.

SOURCE

Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4788, 4793 (June 3, 2011).
