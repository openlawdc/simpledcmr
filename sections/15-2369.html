§ 15-2369. AS FOUND TESTING (§ 15-2369)

2369.1. Meters or associated metering devices, or both, shall be tested in place or after they are removed from service. These tests shall be made before the meters or associated metering devices, or both, are adjusted, repaired, or retired.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 7938, 7948 (November 4, 1988); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 7 (January 4, 2013).
