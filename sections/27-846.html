§ 27-846. DEPARTMENT ASSISTANCE FOR COVERED PROJECTS (§ 27-846)

846.1. If a District agency receives no response to develop a Covered Project from a Qualified Responder, it may contact the Director for further assistance. The Director may:

(a) Assist the District agency to restructure the Covered Project to increase

the likelihood of finding Qualified Responders (for example, determining whether the Covered Project could be separated into components such that one or more portions could be developed by a Development Participant as a Component Developer);

(b) Assist the District agency and its respondents who were not Qualified Respondents in identifying qualified Equity Participants and Development Participants; or

(c) Evaluate a request by the District agency for a modification of some or all of the EDP Program requirements.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 9052 (December 4, 1992); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 8439 (October 23, 2009), incorporating by reference text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 5622, 5653 (July 10, 2009).
