§ 27-815. COMPLIANCE REVIEW (§ 27-815)

815.1 815.1 The Commission or the Department may conduct periodic compliance reviewsf CBEs to assure their continued compliance with the eligibility requirementsf the Act and these regulations.

815.2 815.2 In conducting a compliance review, the Commission or the Department mayerform site visits, review documents, and interview witnesses to determine theontinued eligibility of a CBE.

815.3 815.3 Where a CBE is found to be non-compliant, a notice shall be issued tohe CBE within 30 days which shall specify:

(a) The nature of the non-compliance;

(b) The corrective action that must be taken; and

(c) The date by which the deficiencies must be corrected.

815.4 815.4 If the CBE fails to take corrective action within 30 days of the notice,he Department may institute revocation proceedings.
