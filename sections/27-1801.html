§ 27-1801. DETERMINATION OF REASONABLE PRICE AND AWARD (§ 27-1801)

1801.1 1801.1 The contracting officer shall determine that the price to be paid formall purchases made pursuant to this chapter to the successful offeror is fairnd reasonable.
