§ 29-528. EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS[COLON] MEDICAL DIRECTOR (§ 29-528)

528.1 528.1 Each EMS educational institution offering certification courses shallave a medical director who:

(a) Is a physician licensed to practice medicine in the District of Columbia;

Has current working knowledge and experience in emergency medical care ofcutely ill or injured patients;

(c) Has working knowledge of the District EMS system; and

Has knowledge of the District of Columbia scope of practice for Emergencyedical Services Providers and the National Educational Standards.

528.2 528.2 If the educational institution is a part of an Emergency Medical Servicesgency, the same person may serve as the medical director of both.

528.3 528.3 The medical director shall be responsible for the oversight of theedical educational content of the curriculum.
