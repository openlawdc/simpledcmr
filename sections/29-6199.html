§ 29-6199. DEFINITIONS (§ 29-6199)

6199.1. The following terms and phrases shall have the meanings described:

&#8220;Applicant&#8221; &#8211; A kinship caregiver who is applying for a permanent guardianship subsidy.

&#8220;Application&#8221; &#8211; A written request to be approved for a permanent guardianship subsidy submitted pursuant to this chapter of the DCMR.

&#8220;CFSA&#8221; &#8211; The D.C. Child and Family Services Agency.

&#8220;Child&#8221; &#8211; A person under eighteen (18) years of age.

&#8220;Court&#8221; &#8211; The Family Division of the District of Columbia Superior Court.

&#8220;DCMR&#8221; &#8211; The District of Columbia Municipal Regulations.

&#8220;Foster care board and care payment&#8221; &#8211; Payment provided by CFSA for the provision of basic and routine care to a foster child in a foster home.

&#8220;Kinship caregiver&#8221; &#8211; A person who is licensed or approved by CFSA as a foster parent who provides full-time parenting and protection of a kin foster child.

&#8220;Permanent guardianship&#8221; &#8211; A legal status awarded by the court.

&#8220;Permanent guardianship subsidy&#8221; &#8211; Monies provided by CFSA to a permanent guardian to financially support the care of a child in a permanent guardianship home in accordance with this chapter of the DCMR.

&#8220;Permanent guardianship subsidy agreement&#8221; &#8211; An agreement entered into between CFSA and a permanent guardian concerning the provision of a guardianship subsidy to the permanent guardian.

&#8220;Recipient&#8221; &#8211; A permanent guardian who receives a permanent guardianship subsidy.

SOURCE

Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 3671 (April 27, 2001) [EXPIRED]; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10740 (November 23, 2001).
