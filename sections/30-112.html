§ 30-112. RECORDKEEPING (§ 30-112)

112.1. The records of the Board shall be maintained at the Board&#8217;s Office.

112.2. Transcripts of Board proceedings as required by law or this chapter shall be maintained at the Board&#8217;s office.

SOURCE
