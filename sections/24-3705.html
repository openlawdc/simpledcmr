§ 24-3705. PUBLIC UTILITIES (§ 24-3705)

3705.1. Public utility companies regulated by the Public Service Commission may remove Special Trees in connection with utility construction, line maintenance, and emergency work within the District&#8217;s right-of-way without a Special Tree Removal Permit. Such companies shall comply with the notice requirement set forth in section 105(b) of the Act (D.C. Official Code § 8-651.05(b)) and shall comply with ANSI(a)(300) standards.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 546 (January 21, 2005).
