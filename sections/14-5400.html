§ 14-5400. GENERAL POLICY (§ 14-5400)

5400.1 5400.1 DCHA verifies Family income, Family composition, status of full timetudents, value of assets, factors allowing a preference, and other factorselating to eligibility determinations before an applicant Family is issued aoucher.

5400.2 5400.2 DCHA shall verify all information that is used to establish theamily’s eligibility and level of assistance and DCHA shall obtain theamily’s consent to collect the information. Applicants and participants shallooperate with the verification process as a condition of receiving assistance.

5400.3 5400.3 DCHA shall not pass on the cost of verification to the Family.

5400.4 5400.4 All verification attempts, information obtained, and decisions reacheduring the verification process shall be recorded in the Family’s file inufficient detail to demonstrate that DCHA has followed all of the verificationolicies set forth in this plan. The record should be sufficient to enable aCHA staff member, a HUD reviewer, and a participant to understand the processollowed and conclusions reached.
