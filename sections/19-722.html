§ 19-722. SPONSORED ACTIVITIES[COLON] ASSISTANCE AVAILABLE (§ 19-722)

722.1. When sponsorship is extended by the Department, assistance shall be given by the Department in the following ways:

(a) Issue permit for facilities for rehearsal and for public presentation of the program in the public schools or other facilities available to the Department;

(b) Provide such custodial and supervisory services within its resources as may be agreed upon between the Department and the Organization presenting the program;

(c) Contribute assistance by the Department staff in staging, lighting, decorations, and similar areas; and

(d) Handle all financial procedures through the Operations Division of the Department.

722.2. To ensure full cooperation between the Organization and the Department, and to avoid misunderstandings, a representative of the Department shall attend the business meetings of the advisory board of the Organization.

722.3. Copies of the minutes of business meetings shall be filed with the Department.

SOURCE

15 DCRR § 5.3 (August 10, 1970 and December 23, 1970).
