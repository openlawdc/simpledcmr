§ 29-1605. FUNDING (§ 29-1605)

1605.1. Each child-placing agency shall document that it has sufficient funds to meet all requirements for licensure. The child-placing agency shall assure that sufficient funds are available for a twelve (12) month period of operation.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3035 (May 11, 1990).
