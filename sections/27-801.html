§ 27-801. APPLICATION FOR CERTIFIED BUSINESS ENTERPRISE STATUS (§ 27-801)

801.1 801.1 An applicant for certification with the Department shall submit aompleted application that is signed and sworn to by the applicant, settingorth the basis for certification as a CBE, accompanied by documentationequested by the Department.

801.2 801.2 An applicant for certification, along with the application, shall alsoubmit documentation that includes, but is not limited to:

(a) Business documentation, including articles of incorporation, by-laws,ertificates of good standing, stock voting rights, minutes of organizationaleetings, partnership agreements, profit sharing agreements, buy-out rights,oint venture agreements, tax returns, and annual reports;
