§ 14-1202. REGISTRATION OF TENANTS (§ 14-1202)

1202.1. A licensee shall establish and maintain, within five (5) business days after the opening of the business, a book, books, record, or records in which shall be written in English the name of each tenant of every apartment in the apartment house together with the number of the apartment in which the tenant is residing.

1202.2. The registration book, books, record, or records shall be kept current and in good repair at all times within the District of Columbia, and shall be open for inspection by the departments of the District government responsible for enforcement of District laws and regulations.

SOURCE

The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 6104, Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955); as amended by Commissioners’ Order 57-1023 (June 6, 1957).
