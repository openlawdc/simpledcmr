§ 29-2020. FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FOR PAYMENT OF HEALTH BENEFIT PREMIUMS FOR PERSONS WITHIDS (§ 29-2020)

2020.1. Sections 2020 &#8211; 2024 shall govern the District of Columbia’s program to provide financial assistance in the form of payment of health benefit premiums for certain District of Columbia residents who are disabled due to Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome (AIDS).

2020.2. Initial or continued eligibility for financial assistance under this program shall be contingent upon the availability of funds identified for the program in supporting schedules for the District’s appropriations act.

AUTHORITY

The authority for §2020 &#8211; 2024 is D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. §4-110(3); Pub. L. 101-518; and the report of the Committee of the Whole, D.C. Council, accompanying Bill 8-516, the Fiscal Year 1991 Budget Request Act (March 20, 1990).

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 4360 (July 12, 1991).
