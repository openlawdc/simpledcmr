§ 9-455. PARKING FEES[COLON] RESIDENTS OF APARTMENTS, CONDOMINIUMS, AND COOPS (§ 9-455)

455.1. Residents of apartment houses, condominiums, and cooperatives who park on the same premises which they occupy as a residence shall not require an exemption card.

455.2. For the purposes of this section, the term &#8220;same premises&#8221; means an area within the building or adjacent to the building or premises owned by the apartment landlord, or the condominium or cooperative, for the purpose of providing parking for its residents.

455.3. For the purposes of this section, private individuals who maintain a permanent residence in an apartment hotel, hotel, or motel are considered to be residents of an apartment house.
