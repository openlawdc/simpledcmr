§ 17-5211. STANDARDS OF CONDUCT (§ 17-5211)

5211.1. Any holder of a license under this chapter to practice as a naturopathic physician shall comply with the standards of ethical and professional conduct established by the American Association of Naturopathic Physicians as they may be amended or republished from time to time.

SOURCE

As amended by final rulemaking published at 53 DCR 4799 (June 16, 2006).
