§ 15-1200. RESTRICTIONS (§ 15-1200)

1200.1. Student travel shall be restricted in accordance with the following:

(a) On Saturdays and Sundays throughout the calendar year;

(b) During the following vacations periods:

(1) Christmas: December 25th through January 1st; and

(2) Summer: June 26th through August 31st; and

(c) On other dates, as determined by the Director of Public Works.

1200.2. Metrobus operators shall not accept the regular metallic student bus tokens during the periods cited in this section.

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is the School Transit Subsidy Act of 1978, D.C. Law 2-152, D.C. Code §44-216 et seq. (1981 Ed.).

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 2443 (May 29, 1981), incorporating text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 1834, 1835 (April 24, 1981).
