§ 20-3112. WARNING AND DISCLAIMER OF LIABILITY (§ 20-3112)

3112.1. The degree of flood protection required by this Chapter is considered reasonable for regulatory purposes and is based on scientific and engineering considerations. Larger floods can and will occur and flood heights may be increased by manmade or natural causes. This Chapter does not imply that land outside the identified floodplain areas or SFHAs, or uses permitted within these areas will be free from flooding or flood damages.

3112.2. This Chapter shall not create liability on the part of the District or by any officer or employee thereof for any flood damages that result from reliance on this Chapter or any administrative decision lawfully made there under.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 6547, 6562 (November 15, 1985); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 10781 (November 19, 2010).
