§ 24-4799. DEFINITIONS (§ 24-4799)

4799.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have the following meanings:

Case management services &#8211; Those services that assist an eligible individual in accessing needed medical, social, educational, and other services appropriate to the needs of the individual.

CFSA &#8211; the District of Columbia Child and Family Services Agency.

Client &#8211; a person who is eligible for TCM services.

Medical Assistance Administration (MAA) &#8211; the Single State Agency, within the Department of Health, responsible for administering the District of Columbia Medicaid Program.

Provider &#8211; The District of Columbia Child and Family Services Agency (CFSA) and its subcontractors.

TCM &#8211; Targeted Case Management Services.
