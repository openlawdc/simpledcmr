§ 16-810. RECEIPTS (§ 16-810)

810.1. Prior to the completion of home improvement work, no home improvement contractor shall accept any payment for the work without promptly delivering to the homeowner a receipt for that payment.

810.2. If payment is made by check or U.S. Postal money order, no receipt need be delivered to the homeowner.
