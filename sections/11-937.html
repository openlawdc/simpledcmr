§ 11-937. GROSS FLOOR AREA (W) (§ 11-937)

937.1. A public recreation and community center shall not exceed a gross floor area of forty thousand square feet (40,000 sq. ft.), unless approved by the Board of Zoning Adjustment as a special exception in accordance with the provisions of § 3104.1.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 10137 (November 28, 2003).
