§ 20-599. DEFINITIONS AND ABBREVIATIONS (§ 20-599)

599.1. The meanings ascribed to the definitions and abbreviations appearing in §§ 199.1 and 199.2 respectively of Chapter 1 shall apply to the terms and abbreviations in this chapter.
