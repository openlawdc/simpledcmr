§ 28-2099. DEFINITIONS (§ 28-2099)

2099.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms shall have the meanings ascribed:

Chairperson &#8211; The Chairperson of the Commission, or the Vice Chairperson or Acting Chairperson designated by the Commission when acting as Chairperson.

Evaluation &#8211; The process whereby the Commission, pursuant to §433(c) of the Self-Government Act, prepares and submits to the President of the United States a written report evaluating the performance and fitness of a candidate for reappointment to a District of Columbia court.

Investigation &#8211; an inquiry to determine whether a proceeding should be instituted.

Judge &#8211; a judge, senior judge, or retired judge of the District of Columbia Court of Appeals or of the Superior Court of the District of Columbia.

Proceeding &#8211; a formal proceeding, initiated by a Notice of Proceeding, to hear and determine charges as to a judge&#8217;s conduct or health pursuant to §432 (a)(2) or (b) of the Self-Government Act.

Recommendation &#8211; The process whereby the Commission, pursuant to D.C. Code, Title 11, §11-1504, prepares and submits a written report of its recommendation and findings to the chief judge of a District of Columbia court regarding the appointment of senior judges to the court.

Self-Government Act &#8211; the District of Columbia Self-Government and Governmental Reorganization Act of 1973, Pub. L. 93-198.

Special Counsel &#8211; any member of the District of Columbia Bar retained by the Commission to assist it.
