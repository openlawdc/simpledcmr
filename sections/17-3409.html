§ 17-3409. INACTIVE STATUS (§ 17-3409)

3409.1 3409.1 Upon application by a licensee and payment of the required fee, theoard shall place a licensee on inactive status.

3409.2 3409.2 While on inactive status, an individual shall not practice, attempt toractice, or offer to practice architecture in the District of Columbia.

3409.3 3409.3 A licensee may remain on inactive status for up to five (5) years fromhe date of application, and shall notify the Board of any address change withinhirty (30) days of the change.

3409.4 3409.4 The Board shall issue a license to an individual who is on inactivetatus and who desires to resume practice, if the individual meets the followingequirements:

(a) Files an application with the Board;

(b) Pays the required fees;

(c) Demonstrates compliance with all continuing education requirements; and

(d) Complies with all current requirements for license renewal.

3409.5 3409.5 A lapsed license cannot be placed on inactive status. The license mustirst be reinstated, as provided in D.C. Official Code § 47-2853.15, providedhe license has not been expired for more than five (5) years.
