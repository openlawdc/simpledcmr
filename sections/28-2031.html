§ 28-2031. EVALUATION STANDARDS (§ 28-2031)

2031.1. A judge declaring candidacy for reappointment shall be evaluated by the Commission through a review of the judge&#8217;s performance and conduct during the judge&#8217;s present term of office.

2031.2. The evaluation categories shall include the following:

(a) Well Qualified &#8211; The candidate&#8217;s work product, legal scholarship, dedication, efficiency, and demeanor are exceptional, and the candidate&#8217;s performance consistently reflects credit on the judicial system.

(b) Qualified &#8211; The candidate satisfactorily performs the judicial function or, if there are negative traits, they are overcome by strong positive attributes.

(c) Unqualified &#8211; The candidate is unfit for further judicial service.
