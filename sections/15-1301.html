§ 15-1301. DETERMINATION OF REIMBURSEMENTS (§ 15-1301)

1301.1. Each public utility, competitive electric supplier, competitive natural gas supplier, and competitive local exchange carrier (CLEC) shall be assessed according to D.C. Official Code §34-912(b) (2011 Supp.) for the reimbursable budgets of the Commission and the Office of the People&#8217;s Counsel in the following manner:

(a) For CLECs, competitive electric suppliers, and competitive natural gas suppliers (collectively &#8220;alternative providers&#8221;), the assessments shall be equal to the ratio of the alternative provider&#8217;s calendar year gross revenues to the sum of the calendar year gross revenues of all public utilities and all alternative providers times the budgets of the Commission and the Office of the People’s Counsel; or

(b) For public utilities, the assessment shall be the utility&#8217;s proportionate share of the calendar year gross revenues of all public utilities times the budgets of the Commission and the Office of the People’s Counsel less the amount to be reimbursed by the alternative providers in paragraph (a).

1301.2. On April 15th of each year, each utility, competitive electric supplier, competitive natural gas supplier, and CLEC shall file its gross jurisdictional revenue figures for the preceding calendar year ending December 31st.

SOURCE
