§ 11-2111. SURFACE PARKING LOTS LANDCAPING STANDARDS (§ 11-2111)

2111.1 2111.1 Surface parking areas with ten (10) or more parking spaces shall conformo the landscaping, tree canopy cover, screening, and lighting requirements aset forth in this section:

A minimum of ten percent (10%) of the total area devoted to parking, includingisles and driveways shall be covered by landscaped areas planted with trees andhrubs;

The landscaping shall be maintained in a healthy, growing condition; Dead orying landscaping shall be replaced;

All end islands of parking rows longer than nine (9) parking spaces, and allreas otherwise not used for ingress and egress, aisles, and parking spaceshall be landscaped;

Landscaping around the perimeter of the parking area may count toward the areaequirement of this subsection up to a distance of six feet (6 ft.) from theavement;

All newly planted trees shall have a minimum diameter of two and one-half inches2.5 in.); all trees shall be planted or retained in a space that provides ainimum of five hundred (500) cubic feet of soil volume per tree; and

Trees shall be planted a minimum of four feet (4 ft.) from any protectivearrier, such as curbs or wheel stops with no horizontal dimension less thanour feet (4 ft.) and a minimum depth of three feet (3 ft.).

2111.2 2111.2 The Board of Zoning Adjustment may grant, by special exception, a fullr partial reduction in the landscape standards for parking lots required byhis section if, in addition to meeting the general requirements of § 3104, thepplicant demonstrates that complying with the landscape standards ismpractical because of size of lot, or other conditions relating to the lot orurrounding area that would tend to make full compliance unduly restrictive,rohibitively costly, or unreasonable.
