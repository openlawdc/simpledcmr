§ 24-2318. MODIFICATION OF CERTIFICATES (§ 24-2318)

2318.1 2318.1 If the information contained in the certificate is no longer accurateue to the holder’s changed circumstances, the holder shall, in accordanceith § 208 of the Act (D.C. Official Code § 7-2502.08 (2013 Supp.)), submithe certificate and a statement concerning the changes.

2318.2 2318.2 A duplicate certificate showing the changes as reported shall be issuedithout charge.

2318.3 2318.3 Issuance of the duplicate certificate shall automatically invalidate thereviously held certificate.
