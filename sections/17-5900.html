§ 17-5900. APPLICABILITY (§ 17-5900)

5900.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a certificate to practice nurse-practitioners.

5900.2. Chapters 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules), 41 (Health Occupations: Administrative Procedures), and 54 (Registered Nursing) of this title shall supplement this chapter.

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is § 302(14) of the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of 1985, D.C. Law 6-99, D.C. Code § 2-3303.2(14) (1988 Repl. Vol.), 33 DCR 729, 732 (February 7, 1986), and Mayor&#8217;s Order 86-110, 33 DCR 5220 (August 22, 1986).

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 3505 (May 12, 1989).
