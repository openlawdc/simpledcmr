§ 19-209. SICK LEAVE (§ 19-209)

209.1 209.1 All regular full-time employees shall earn eight (8) hours of sick leaveer month. All regular full-time employees begin to accrue leave starting withheir first day of employment. Accrual for regular part-time employees andmployees in non-pay status shall be prorated to correspond with the actualumber of hours worked.

209.2 209.2 All regular part-time employees shall earn sick leave by the same methods regular full-time employees, provided that the amount earned shall berorated to correspond with the actual number of hours worked.

209.3 209.3 An employee may use sick leave for personal illness or to care for aelative and other children and adults for whom the employee is a primaryaretaker.

The Authority shallay the cost of such a medical examination.

209.5 209.5 An employee who uses all of his or her accrued sick leave and annualeave may be placed on leave of absence without pay status for the duration ofhe illness of the employee, relative, or other child or adult for whom themployee is the primary caretaker.
