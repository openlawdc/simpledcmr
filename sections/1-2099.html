§ 1-2099. DEFINITIONS (§ 1-2099)

2099.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms shall have the meanings ascribed:

Care &#8211; Treatment, services, or benefits provided to an injured or diseased policeman or fireman.

Policeman or Fireman &#8211; Any police officer employed by the Metropolitan Police Department of firefighter employed by the District of Columbia Fire Department or the personal representative or attorney of that police officer or firefighter.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 798, 801-02 (January 27, 1989).
