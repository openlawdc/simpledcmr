§ 18-2415. FEES FOR STICKERS OR PERMITS (§ 18-2415)

2415.1. The Director may establish a fee for residential permit parking stickers, visitor permits, and temporary permits.

2415.2. The Director may establish a fee for the replacement of stickers or permits, but the fee may not exceed the cost of the original sticker or permit.

2415.3. The fee for a one-year residential permit parking sticker shall be $35 annually per vehicle per legal-mailing address, except permits issued to residents 65 years of age or older shall be $25 annually.

Source: Section 2(m)(1) of the Residential Permit Parking Regulation Amendment Act of 1984, D.C. Law 5-185, 32 DCR 859, 868 (February 15, 1985); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 2759 (May 10, 2991); as amended by section 6092 of the Fiscal Year 2012 Budget Support Act of 2011, effective September 14, 2011 (D.C. Law 19-21), published at 58 DCR 6226, 6300 (July 29, 2011).
