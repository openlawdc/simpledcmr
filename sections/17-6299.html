§ 17-6299. DEFINITIONS (§ 17-6299)

6299.1. As used in this chapter, the following terms have the meanings ascribed:

A.I.T. &#8211; a person enrolled in a board-approved Administrator In Training program in a licensed nursing facility under a licensed nursing home administrator preceptor.

Board &#8211; the Board of Nursing Home Administration, established by § 205 of the Act (D.C. Official Code § 3-1202.05) (2001 ed.).

NABENHA &#8211; the National Association of Boards of Examiners for Nursing Home Administrators.

Preceptor &#8211; a licensed nursing home administrator with at least three (3) years of experience who is approved by the Board to serve as a preceptor.

Supervisor &#8211; A licensed nursing home administrator (LNHA), person designated by the administrator, assistant nursing home administrator, or department head who has a defining role pursuant to § 6202.4 of this chapter.

6299.2. The definitions in § 4099 of Chapter 40 of this title are incorporated by reference into and apply to this chapter.
