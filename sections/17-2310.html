§ 17-2310. CONTINUING EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS (§ 17-2310)

2310.1 2310.1 Except as provided in § 2310.2, this section shall apply to allpplicants for the renewal or reinstatement of each license classification.

2310.2 2310.2 An applicant for renewal or reinstatement of a real estate appraisericense, or renewal of an appraiser trainee license, shall be required toatisfactorily complete continuing education courses according to the followingchedule:

(a) Applicants obtaining a license one hundred eighty-five (185) days or lessrom the renewal date required by the license cycle are not required to completeny hours of continuing education for that license cycle;

(b) Applicants obtaining a license during the last twelve (12) months of aicense cycle, but before the one hundred eighty-five (185) day period mustomplete fourteen (14) hours of continuing education for that license cycle;

(c) All other licensees must complete the full twenty-eight (28) hours ofontinuing education for the license cycle; and

(d) All licensees must complete the seven- (7-) hour National USPAP Updateourse as part of the twenty-eight (28) hours of continuing education.

2310.3 2310.3 [RESERVED]

2310.4 2310.4 The Board will grant credit toward the classroom hour requirement onlyhen the length of the educational offering is at least three (3) hours.

2310.5 2310.5 The Board may approve the following continuing education activities, noto exceed (10) hours, if the activity relates to real estate appraising:

(a) Serving as an instructor or speaker at a conference, seminar, or workshop;nd

(b) Publication of an article in a professional journal, publication of a bookr a chapter in a book, or publication of a review in a professional journal.

2310.6 2310.6 A continuing education credit shall be valid only if it is a part of aoard-approved program or activity.

2310.7 2310.7 [RESERVED]

2310.8 2310.8 Continuing education credit for the National USPAP Update Course shallnly be awarded to licensees completing USPAP continuing education coursesaught by an AQB-certified instructor who is also a certified appraiser.
