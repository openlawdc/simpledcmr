§ 29-5410. USE OF INFORMATION AND DATA (§ 29-5410)

5410.1. The Department shall have a license, free of charge, to use any data or information system, including software, documentation and manuals, developed by the fee-for-service primary care provider pursuant to the implementation of the provider’s Medicaid managed care provider agreement.

5410.2. Data, information, and reports collected or prepared by the fee-for-service primary care provider in the course of carrying out his or her obligations under the Medicaid managed care provider agreement shall not be used by the fee-for-service primary care provider for any purpose not directly related to meeting the terms of the Medicaid managed care provider agreement without the prior written permission of the Department.

5410.3. The Department may reproduce, publish and otherwise use and authorize others to use, for District, state or federal government purposes, any material developed by the fee-for-service provider in the course of the performance of the Medicaid managed care provider agreement.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 1566, 1594 (March 31, 1995).
