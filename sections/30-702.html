§ 30-702. OFFICIAL END OF INSTANT GAMES (§ 30-702)

702.1. The official end of any instant game shall be the date announced by the Executive Director. For the purposes of §600.4, the drawing date of a particular instant game shall be the date of the official end of the game.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7439, 7450 (October 2, 1992).

HISTORICAL NOTE

Prior to October 2, 1992, the D.C. Charitable Games Control Board published Final Rulemakings at 29 DCR 2202, 2212 (May 28, 1982).
