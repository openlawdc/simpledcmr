§ 19-211. HEALTH INSURANCE, LIFE INSURANCE AND RETIREMENT (§ 19-211)

211.1 211.1 Regular full-time and regular part-time employees shall be eligible fornrollment on a single or dependent basis in one (1) or more group healthnsurance plans selected by the Authority.

211.2 211.2 The Authority shall contribute a share, determined by the Board ofirectors, of the cost of such single or dependent health insurance coverage foregular full-time employees. The Authority&#8217;s contribution for regularart-time employees shall be a prorated amount, which is based on the actualumber of hours worked per week by a regular part-time employee.

211.3 211.3 Regular full-time employees shall be eligible for enrollment in a lifensurance plan selected by the Authority.

211.4 211.4 The Authority shall pay the cost of such life insurance coverage foregular full-time employees and a portion of the cost for regular part-timemployees.

211.5 211.5 The Authority shall not contribute to an employee’s health coverage orife insurance if the employee is on general or military leave for more thanhirty (30) days.

211.6 211.6 Regular full-time and regular part-time employees shall participate in anmployees’ Retirement Plan approved by the Board of Directors. In addition,asual employees who work at least one thousand (1,000) hours in a “retirementlan year” shall become eligible for participation. The Authority shallontribute to the Employees&#8217; Retirement Plan.
