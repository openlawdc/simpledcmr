§ 17-8500. GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 17-8500)

8500.1 8500.1 These rules are promulgated pursuant to the “Licensed Health Professional Criminal Background Check Amendment Act of 2006”, effective March 6, 2007 (D.C. Law 16-222, D.C. Official Code § 3-1205.22 et seq.)(hereinafter “the Act”).

8500.2. Chapters 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules) and 41 (Health Occupations: Administrative Procedures) shall supplement this chapter.

Source: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 6025 (July 31, 2009).
