§ 30-800. CONDUCT OF ON-LINE GAMES (§ 30-800)

800.1. The Agency may operate any of the on-line games described in Chapter 9 of this title.

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is §4 of the Law to Legalize Lotteries, Daily Numbers Games, and Bingo and Raffles for Charitable Purposes in the District &#038;Columbia, D.C. Law 3-172. D.C. Official Code §3-1301 et seq.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7439, 7450 (October 2, 1992).
