§ 31-1208. LICENSING OF LCS VEHICLE OPERATORS – INVESTIGATION, EXAMINATION, ANDDUCATION REQUIREMENTS (§ 31-1208)

1208.1 1208.1 Upon receipt of an application for a license to operate, the Office shall investigate each applicant to verify the identity and determine the competency, fitness, and eligibility of the applicant for a license.

1208.2. Each applicant shall attend and complete such courses of training and education as the Office requires, and shall successfully pass such tests as the Office requires as conditions for licensing.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 2812, 2820 (May 20, 1994); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 5298 (July 4, 2003); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 12394 (August 30, 2013).
