§ 29-4235. INCIDENTS AND COMPLAINTS (§ 29-4235)

4235.1. Each provider of waiver services shall document, report, investigate, and resolve all incidents and complaints. Each provider of waiver services shall forward a copy of each incident report or complaint to the MAA and shall maintain a copy of all incidents and complaints on file for a period of not less than six (6) years.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 9025 (October 24, 2003).
