§ 21-509. CORRECTION OF CURRENT EROSION PROBLEMS (§ 21-509)

509.1 509.1 In instances where erosion is occurring as the result of natural forces or past land-disturbing activities, but in the absence of current land-disturbing activities, the Department shall have the authority to inspect the site and to order the property owner to correct the erosion problem.

509.2 509.2 Each order to correct existing problems shall specify the general corrective measures to be applied.

509.3. The Department shall maintain and provide to homeowners who are required to correct erosion problems information relating to possible sources of financial assistance for the project.

SOURCE

Section 2 of the Water Quality Standard Act of 1977 (D.C Law 2-68, 24 DCR 6809 (February 17, 1978) 8 DCRR § 8-2.809 Health Regulations (1965 Edition); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 10640 (July 19, 2013).
