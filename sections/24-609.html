§ 24-609. PARKING FACILITY SIGNS AND NOTICE OF PARKING FEES (§ 24-609)

609.1 609.1 In all areas other than those zoned only for residential use, eachicensee shall place and maintain a legible and conspicuous sign that is clearlyisible at every entrance to the licensed premises. Each sign shall state theame of the licensee and the hours of the day and night during which theremises are open and attended for the storage of vehicles; and it shall complyith all applicable regulations in force in the District of Columbia.

609.2 609.2 Each sign shall also state, in a clearly visible manner, the hourly andaily rates charged for the storage of vehicles on the paid premises.

609.3 609.3 No licensee shall charge, or permit to be charged, any greater fee thans posted at the time of entry. Rates applicable to weekly or monthly contracts,owever, need not be posted.

609.4 609.4 The provisions of this section shall also apply to parking facilitiesocated in residential zones, except that each sign shall conform to the signegulations applicable to residential zones.

SOURCE

Article 28, §2(k) of the Police Regulations (1981) (as section 612);s amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 7303 (September 4, 2009).
