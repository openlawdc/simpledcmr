§ 19-203. RELATIVES (§ 19-203)

203.1 203.1 The Authority shall not hire relatives of members of the Board ofirectors or of employees. Any exceptions are subject to the prior review andpproval of the President and Chief Executive Officer. In an instance where anxception is approved, the related employees shall not work in the sameepartment, or be supervised by one another.

203.2 203.2 If an employee of the Authority becomes related (by marriage, adoption,r any other means) to a member of the Board of Directors or another employeeho is his or her supervisor or works in the same department, the President andhief Executive Officer shall:

(a) Require an employee who becomes related to a member of the Board ofirectors to resign; and

(b) Require one (1) of the two (2) employees who become related to be reassignedo another position if an appropriate vacancy is available or require one of themployees to resign.

203.3 203.3 An employee so affected shall have six (6) months to be reassigned or toesign.
