§ 29-5833. NEEDS COVERED BY DIVERSION PAYMENTS (§ 29-5833)

5833.1. Diversion payments are available to pay an immediate, short term financial need, which if resolved, will allow the applicant to continue or accept employment.

5833.2. An immediate, short-term financial need for which an applicant may receive a Diversion Payment (up to the maximum amount) includes, but is not limited to:

(a) Motor vehicle insurance;

(b) Repair of a motor vehicle, but not including routine maintenance such as oil change, windshield wiper blades, air conditioner repair, etc.;

(c) Rent, mortgage payments, security deposit and utility arrearage(s), but not utilities alone;

(d) Work clothes such as uniform, special shoes, etc.;

(e) Special work tools; and

(f) Required licenses and fees.
