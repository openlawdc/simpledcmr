§ 29-517. EMS PROVIDERS – EMERGENCY MEDICAL TECHNICIAN (§ 29-517)

517.1 517.1 Each applicant for certification as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT)hall submit an application to the Director. The application shall include:

A copy of a valid NREMT-EMT (or greater) certification card;

(b) A copy of a valid AHA Health Care Provider (or equivalent) CPR card;

(c) A completed application signed by the sponsoring EMS agency’s medicalirector; and

(d) A check or money order made out in the appropriate fee made payable to the.C. Treasurer (if applicable).

517.2 517.2 Upon receipt of a complete application, the Director shall review thepplication and, if approved, shall issue a D.C. EMT certification card.
