§ 21-5330. METHODS OF PROCUREMENT (§ 21-5330)

5330.1 5330.1 Authority procurements shall be conducted using a method or combinationf methods, which:

Serve the Authority’s interest considering price, delivery, quality, effect onhe Authority’s operation and services, and other factors affecting theuthority’s interests; and

Deal fairly with offerors and contractors.

5330.2 5330.2 The Authority may use any of the following methods of procurement:

Sealed Bids;

Competitive Proposals;

Small Purchases;

Commercial Item Purchases;

Expedited Purchases;

Limited Competition Purchases;

Joint Procurements;

Rider Procurements;

General Services Administration Schedule Purchases;

Micro-Purchases; or

Unsolicited Proposals.
