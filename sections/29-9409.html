§ 29-9409. SITE VISITS (§ 29-9409)

9409.1. In accordance with 42 C.F.R. § 455.432, DHCF shall conduct unannounced, pre-enrollment and post-enrollment site visits of providers and suppliers who are designated as “Moderate Risk” or “High Risk.”

9409.2. DHCF shall reserve the right to conduct unannounced site visits of any provider or supplier enrolled in the Medicaid program.

9409.3. Applicants and enrolled providers and suppliers shall be required to permit on-site inspections to be conducted by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, including CMS, the Department of Health (“DOH”), DHCF, or any designee selected by any of the aforementioned. Site visits shall be used to verify that the information submitted to DHCF is accurate and to assess providers and suppliers’ compliance with all applicable Federal and District laws and regulations.

SOURCE
