§ 16-1800. GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 16-1800)

1800.1. The provisions of this chapter are adopted under the authority of D.C. Law 3-107, the &#8220;Security Alarm Systems Regulations Act of 1980,&#8221; D.C. Code § 7-2801 et seq. (2001), effective September 26, 1980, as amended by D.C. Law 7-99, the &#8220;Fire Alarm Systems Regulations Amendments Act of 1977,&#8221; effective March 29, 1988 (hereafter referred to as the &#8220;Act&#8221;).

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is the Security Alarm Systems Regulations Act of 1980, D.C. Law 3-107, D.C. Code § 7-2801 et seq. (2001), as amended by the Fire Alarm Systems Regulations Amendments Act of 1987, D.C. Law 7-99.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8448 (December 2, 1988).
