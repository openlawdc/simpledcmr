§ 17-6209. LICENSURE BY RECIPROCITY (§ 17-6209)

6209.1. The Board may issue a license to an applicant who:

(a) Is currently licensed and in good standing under the laws of another state with standards which are substantially equivalent to the requirements of the District of Columbia laws and regulations;

(b) Has at least three (3) years of current practice as a nursing home administrator;

(c) Has passed the District examination;

(d) Is licensed in a state which admits health professionals in a like manner to the District of Columbia;

(e) Is licensed in a state that has an agreement between the state and the District&#8217;s Board;

(f) Pays the applicable fees established by the Mayor;

(g) Has been interviewed by the Board regarding the applicant&#8217;s past and present experience relating to the practice of nursing home administration; and

(h) Has forwarded to the Board at least three (3) letters of recommendation by persons who can attest to the applicant&#8217;s qualifications as they pertain to the practice of nursing home administration.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 10629 (November 19, 2004).
