§ 19-729. USE FOR FUND RAISING BY COMMUNITY PROHIBITED (§ 19-729)

729.1. The use of public recreational facilities shall not be granted for commercial uses, for private profit, or for money-raising purposes.

729.2. The provisions of this section shall not preclude the Department from raising funds for the payment of necessary expenses of community and recreational activities which the Department organizes and directs as an integral part of its program.

729.3. The provisions of this section shall not preclude the use of public recreation facilities by programs to provide community access to healthy foods, such as farmers’ markets.

SOURCE

15 DCRR § 6.6 (August 10, 1970 and December 23, 1970); as amended by the Healthy Schools Act of 2010, effective July 27, 2010 (D.C. Law 18-209), published at 57 DCR 7548 (August 20, 2010).
