§ 19-102. BOARD OF DIRECTORS (§ 19-102)

102.1 102.1 The direction, control and management of the affairs and funds of theuthority shall be vested in the Board, which shall pursue such policies andctivities as shall be in accordance with the provisions of the Act and theelevant statutes of the United States and the District of Columbia. The Boardill employ staff and adopt appropriate procedures to carry out its duties.

102.2 102.2 After notice, the Mayor of the District of Columbia shall remove anyember for failure to establish or maintain residency in the District ofolumbia as required by the Act, or for misconduct or neglect of duty as definedy Section 199 of these bylaws.

102.3 102.3 A Member may resign at any time by giving notice thereof in writing tohe Mayor, with a copy to the Chairperson. The Chairperson may resign at anyime by giving notice thereof in writing to the Mayor, with copies to the Vicehairperson and the Secretary.
