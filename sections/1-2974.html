§ 1-2974. PUBLIC BENEFITS CASES – SUBPOENAS (§ 1-2974)

2974.1 2974.1 Any party may file a request in writing for an Administrative Law Judgeo issue a subpoena to require a witness to attend a hearing.

2974.2 2974.2 The Administrative Law Judge shall issue a subpoena under this Sectionf it is likely that the witness will be able to provide testimony that will beelpful in deciding the case, and if requiring the witness to appear will not benduly burdensome, or otherwise contrary to law.

2974.3 2974.3 Any party also may request a subpoena to require a witness to bringocuments, photographs, or other things to the hearing. The Administrative Lawudge will issue a subpoena if it is likely that the requested items will beelpful in deciding the case, and if requiring those items to be produced willot be unduly burdensome, or otherwise contrary to law.

2974.4 2974.4 A form to be used to request a subpoena is available from OAH.

2974.5 2974.5 If an Administrative Law Judge issues a subpoena, the party requestinghe subpoena must deliver it pursuant to Subsections 2824.6 through 2824.9 and824.11. Unless otherwise ordered by an Administrative Law Judge, deliveryhall be made at least two (2) days before the hearing.
