§ 17-3127. HEARING NOTICE PROCEDURES (§ 17-3127)

3127.1 3127.1 If a respondent requests a hearing, the Director shall notify theespondent of the date, time, and place of the hearing within twenty (20) daysollowing receipt of the request.

3127.2 3127.2 The Director shall hold the hearing not less than fifteen (15) daysollowing the date of the mailing or delivery of the notice, unless the Directornd all of the parties agree to the holding of the hearing at an earlier date.

Source: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7853, 7868 (October 23, 1992).
