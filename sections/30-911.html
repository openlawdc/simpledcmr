§ 30-911. DESCRIPTION OF THE D.C. QUICK CASH GAME (§ 30-911)

911.1. The Agency may operate any one of the following D.C. QUICK CASH games by which players select the requisite numbers out of the total numbers:

(a) Five (5) numbers out of forty (40) possible numbers;

(b) Five (5) numbers out forty-five (45) possible numbers;

(c) Five (5) numbers out of fifty-two (52) possible numbers;

(d) Six (6) numbers out of thirty-one (31) possible numbers;

(e) Six (6) numbers out of thirty-three (33) possible numbers;

(f) Six (6) numbers out of thirty-six (36) possible numbers;

(g) Six (6) numbers out of thirty-nine (39) possible numbers;

(h) Six (6) numbers out of forty (40) possible numbers; or

(i) Six (6) numbers out of forty-five (45) possible numbers.

911.2. The price of a QUICK CASH game ticket shall be three (3) boards for one dollar ($1) or any other price designated by the Executive Director from a price schedule adopted by the Board pursuant to §500.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7439, 7463 (October 2, 1992).
