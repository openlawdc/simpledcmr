§ 30-610. [RESERVED] (§ 30-610)

610.1. Except as provided in writing and approved by the Board or Executive Director prior to the adoption of this section, or as provided in § 908.19 or § 932.1(b), instant, on-line, annuitized or lump sum cash prize payments remaining upon the death of a prize winner shall be payable to the estate of the deceased prize winner by the payment method to which the decedent was entitled at the time the winning game ticket was purchased.

610.2. In the event of the death of a natural person who was the sole recipient of a prize designated pursuant to § 601.1, the Executive Director may determine the proper recipient of any remaining prize payments or may seek a determination of the lawful payee pursuant to § 609.2.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 1438 (March 13, 1998); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 872 (February 1, 2002).
