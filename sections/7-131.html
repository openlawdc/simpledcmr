§ 7-131. REPRESENTATION (§ 7-131)

131.1 131.1 Any claimant who wishes to be represented in any proceeding before an

ALJ under the Act shall submit a written appointment of the individual who he orhe is authorizing to undertake the representation to the OHA or shall make theppointment on the record at a hearing.

131.2 131.2 A duly appointed representative may make or give, on behalf of thelaimant he or she represents, any request or notice relative to any proceedingefore the OHA under the Act, including formal hearing and review.

131.3 131.3 A representative shall be entitled to present or elicit evidence and makellegations as to facts and law in any proceeding affecting the claimant he orhe represents and to obtain information with respect to the claim of thelaimant.

131.4 131.4 A copy of a notice to any claimant of any administrative action,etermination, or decision or request to any party for the production ofvidence shall be sent to the representative of the claimant, and the copy ofhe notice or request shall have the same force and effect as if it had beenent to the claimant.
