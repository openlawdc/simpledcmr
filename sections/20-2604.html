§ 20-2604. DATA REQUIREMENTS (§ 20-2604)

2604.1. A manufacturers shall retain and make data available as follows:

(a) Retain data from tests conducted on cigarettes that are offered for sale in the District pursuant to this chapter for three (3) years; and

2. Make data available to the Chief and the Attorney General upon written request to verify compliance with the performance standard required under § 2602.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 10012 (September 26, 2008).
