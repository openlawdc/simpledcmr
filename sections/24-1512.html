§ 24-1512. RETURN OF ANIMAL ON CONDITION OF PAYMENT (§ 24-1512)

1512.1. WHS may require the owner to pay the reasonable expenses incurred by WHS during the custodial period. The reasonableness of the expenses shall be determined by the President or his or her designee, as set forth in § 1509.2(b)(3).

1512.2. WHS may retain custody of the animal until the owner reimburses WHS for such expenses.

Source: Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 316 (January 9, 2009)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published 56 DCR 2374, 2383 (March 27, 2009).
