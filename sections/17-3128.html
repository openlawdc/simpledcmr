§ 17-3128. SERVICE (§ 17-3128)

3128.1 3128.1 A notice, pleading, order, or decision required to be served on a partyhall be served on the party or representative designated by the party or by lawo accept service of papers. If a party has appeared through counsel, servicehall be made upon the counsel of record.

3128.2 3128.2 Service on a party shall be directed to the last known address of thearty on file with the Director and shall be completed by one of the followingethods:

(a) By certified mail, return receipt requested;

(b) By personal delivery;

(c) By delivery to the address of the party, or party’s counsel or agent, onile with the Department, by leaving it with a person of suitable discretion ateast sixteen (16) years of age who is employed or resides at the address; or

(d) As directed by order of the Director.

3128.3 3128.3 Service on the Director or on the Corporation Counsel shall be byertified mail, return receipt requested, or by personal delivery.

3128.4 3128.4 Proof of service, or attempted service, stating the name and address ofhe person on who service is made and the manner and date of service, shall behown by one of the following:

(a) If service is by certified mail, the return receipt;

(b) If service is by personal delivery, the certificate of the server; or

(c) If service is pursuant to an order of the Director, in the manner providedn that order.

Source: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7853, 786 (October 23, 1992).
