§ 16-3106. ADJUDICATION BY MAIL (§ 16-3106)

3106.1. Adjudication by mail shall be available only to a respondent who answers an initial NOI in a timely manner and admits the infraction with explanation.

3106.2. A respondent who admits an infraction with explanation shall submit with the NOI all evidence of mitigating circumstances or other evidence relevant to the respondent&#8217;s explanation.
