§ 29-565. FEES (§ 29-565)

565.1 565.1 The Director shall collect the following fees from applicants for certification as basic life support providers (Emergency Medical Responders and Emergency Medical Technicians) and for provisional and replacement cards:

Application fee (initial and renewal) &#8211; $15;

Initial certification fee &#8211; $30;

Renewal of certification fee &#8211; $30;

Provisional cards &#8211; $10;

Replacement cards &#8211; $10; and

Out of State processing fee – Application fee plus ten dollars ($10).

565.2 565.2 The initial certification fee under § 565.1 shall be prorated based on the length of initial certification as follows:

(a) One (1) to ninety (90) days &#8211; $3.75;

(b) Ninety-one (91) to one hundred eighty (180) days &#8211; $7.50;

(c) One hundred eighty-one (181) to two hundred seventy (270) days &#8211; $11.25;

(d) Two hundred seventy-one (271) to three hundred sixty-five (365) days &#8211; $15;

(e) Three hundred sixty-six (366) to four hundred fifty-five (455) days &#8211; $18.75;

(f) Four hundred fifty-six (456) to five hundred forty-five (545) days &#8211; $22.50;

(g) Five hundred forty-six (546) to six hundred thirty-five (635) days &#8211; $26.25; and

(h) Six hundred thirty-six (636) to seven hundred thirty (730) days &#8211; $30.

565.3. The Director shall collect the following fees from applicants for certification as advanced life support providers (Advanced Emergency Medical Technicians, Emergency Medical Technician-Intermediates, and Paramedics) and for provisional and replacement cards:

Application fee &#8211; $25;

Initial certification fee &#8211; $50;

Renewal of certification fee &#8211; $30;

Provisional cards &#8211; $10;

Replacement cards &#8211; $10; and

Out of State processing fee – Application fee plus ten dollars ($10).

565.4. Certification fee shall be prorated based on the length of initial certification as follows:

(a) One (1) to ninety (90) days &#8211; $6.25;

(b) Ninety-one (91) to one hundred eighty (180) days &#8211; $12.50;

One hundred eighty-one (181) to two hundred seventy (270) days &#8211; $18.75

(d) Two hundred seventy-one (271) to three hundred sixty-five (365) days &#8211; $25;

(e) Three hundred sixty-six (366) to four hundred fifty-five (455) days &#8211; $31.25;

(f) Four hundred fifty-six (456) to five hundred forty-five (545) days &#8211; $37.50;

(g) Five hundred forty-six (546) to six hundred thirty-five (635) days &#8211; $43.75; and

(h) Six hundred thirty-six (636) to seven hundred thirty (730) days &#8211; $50.

565.5. The Director shall collect the following fees for inspecting and certifying ambulances, including Basic Life Support ambulances and Advanced Life Support ambulances:

Advanced Life Support ambulances &#8211; $600;

Basic Life Support ambulances &#8211; $400; and

Follow-up inspections of ambulances following a failed inspection &#8211; $50.

565.6 565.6 The Director shall collect the following fees for certifying Emergency Medical Service instructors:

Application fee &#8211; $5;

Initial certification fee &#8211; $15; and

Renewal of certification fee &#8211; $15.

565.7 565.7 The Director shall collect the following fees for certifying emergency medical services educational institutions:

Application fee &#8211; $100;

Initial certification fee &#8211; $150; and

Renewal of certification fee &#8211; $200.
