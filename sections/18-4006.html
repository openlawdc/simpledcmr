§ 18-4006. BUS STREETS (§ 18-4006)

4006.1. No vehicles shall travel on bus streets except buses proceeding on designated bus routes, bicycles, personal mobility devices, or authorized emergency vehicles, except as otherwise provided in this section.

4006.2. No vehicles shall travel on bus streets except buses proceeding on a designated bus route, bicycles, or authorized emergency vehicles, except as otherwise provided in this section.

4006.3. Single block portions of bus streets may be used by vehicles delivering or picking up goods within the limits of such portions where direct access from adjacent streets or alleys is not provided.
