§ 17-2899. DEFINITIONS (§ 17-2899)

2899.1. As used in this chapter, the following terms have the meanings ascribed:

Act &#8211; the District of Columbia Board of Veterinary Practice Act of 1982, D.C. Law 4-171, D.C. Code §§2-2721 to 2-2737 (1987 Supp.).

Applicant &#8211; a person applying for a license to practice veterinary medicine under this chapter.

Board &#8211; the District of Columbia Board of Veterinary Examiners for the District of Columbia, established by the Act.

Chairperson &#8211; the Chairperson of the Board of Veterinary Examiners designated by the Mayor pursuant to §6(k) of the Act, D.C. Code §2-2725(k) (1987 Supp.), or a person designated by the board to preside at a hearing or act in place of the Chairperson.

Veterinarian &#8211; a person licensed to practice veterinary medicine under this chapter.

Veterinary Assistant &#8211; an person who is trained in animal care and who performs duties under the supervision of a veterinarian.

2899.2. The definitions in §3399 of chapter 33 of this title are incorporated by reference into and are applicable to this title.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 2226, 2239 (March 25, 1988).
