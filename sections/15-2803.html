§ 15-2803. DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA UNIVERSAL SERVICE TRUST FUND (§ 15-2803)

2803.1 2803.1 Funds from the DC USTF will be used to support the enumerated servicesisted in § 2802.1. The funds shall be used to reimburse eligibleelecommunications carriers (ETCs) for the reasonable investments and expensesot recovered from the federal universal service low-income fund.

2803.2 2803.2 The amount to be reimbursed shall be calculated for each ETC to be theemainder of the ETC’s retail tariffed rate less funding from the Federalniversal Service Low Income Fund less the tariffed lifeline rate for eachligible customer subscribing to the ETC’s lifeline service, not to exceed sixollars and fifty cents ($6.50) for each eligible customer. For ETCs that have universal service program for low-income seniors, the amount to be reimbursedhall be similarly calculated, not to exceed eight dollars and fifty cents$8.50).
