§ 20-2100. PURPOSE AND GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 20-2100)

2100.1. The purpose of this chapter is to regulate tobacco sales and smoking, as required by the District of Columbia Smoking Restriction Act of 1979, as amended, and the Department of Health Functions Clarification Act of 2001, as amended, (the “Acts”).

2100.2. This chapter shall apply to all public places and places of employment and, as of 12:01 A.M. on January 2, 2007, the following:

(a) A brew pub;

(b) A club;

(c) A nightclub;

(d) A tavern; and

(e) The bar and bar area of a restaurant.

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is § 6 of the District of Columbia Smoking Restriction Act of 1979, D.C. Law 3-22, as amended by the District of Columbia Smoking Restriction Act of 1979 Amendment Act of 1987, and the Smoking Regulation Amendment Act of 1990, D.C. Code § 6-913 of seq (1995 Repl. Vol.).

SOURCE

As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 5926, 5927 (August 7, 1992); Chapter 15 renumbered as Chapter 21. As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 10623 (November 2, 2007).
