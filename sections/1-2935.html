§ 1-2935. RENTAL HOUSING CASES – REPRESENTATION (§ 1-2935)

2935.1 2935.1 Persons authorized to appear before OAH by Sections 2833 and 2835 mayepresent parties in rental housing cases.

2935.2 2935.2 A tenant association may represent one or more tenants in any proceedings follows:

(a) A statement must be filed with OAH stating that the tenant consents toepresentation by the tenant association and the tenant association consents toepresent the tenant;

(b) A tenant or a tenant association may revoke the consent by filing atatement to that effect;

(c) A tenant association shall designate one (1) or more members or attorneys toepresent the association and any of the tenants it represents;

(d) A tenant association may elect to proceed only in a representative capacityithout being listed as a party or listed in the caption.

2935.3 2935.3 The provisions of Sections 2833 and 2835 concerning discipline ofersons appearing before OAH apply to all representatives in rental housingases.

2935.4 2935.4 If an Administrative Law Judge decides that a proceeding is so complex,r the potential liability so great that a party should be represented by aawyer, the Administrative Law Judge shall explain to the party the advantagesf obtaining a lawyer and offer to continue the case to give the party anpportunity to obtain a lawyer.
