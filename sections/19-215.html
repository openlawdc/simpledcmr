§ 19-215. CHANGES IN EMPLOYMENT STATUS (§ 19-215)

215.1 215.1 When an employee changes from casual status to either regular part-timer regular full-time status, the employee shall begin earning sick leave andnnual leave from the date of the change in status.

215.2 215.2 When an employee changes from casual or regular part-time status toegular full-time status, the employee shall be eligible for standard lifensurance benefits.

215.3 215.3 An employee who terminates his or her employment with the Authority ands later rehired within six (6) months of resignation shall receive full creditor prior employment in determining annual leave accrual and other fringeenefits.

215.4 215.4 The Authority shall treat employees rehired after a period of six (6)onths as new employees for the purposes of determining annual leave accrual andther fringe benefits.
