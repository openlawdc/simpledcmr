§ 27-803. DISADVANTAGED BUSINESS ENTERPRISE; ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS (§ 27-803)

803.1 803.1 An applicant seeking certification as a Disadvantaged Businessnterprise (“DBE”), in addition to satisfying the requirements of a CBE,hall demonstrate to the Department that the individual(s) representing morehan fifty percent (50%) of those who own, operate, and control the businessnterprise are:

(a) Socially disadvantaged because the individuals have faced chronic,on-fleeting instances of prejudice or bias without regard to their qualities asndividuals over which they have no control due to their identity as members of group as evidenced by the following:

(1) Documentation proving that the individuals seeking socially disadvantagedtatus as members of a group hold themselves out as members of that group;

(2) Documentation proving that the individuals seeking socially disadvantagedtatus have been isolated from the mainstream of American society in ways notommon to business persons generally; and

(3) Documentation proving that the individuals seeking socially disadvantagedtatus have personally suffered social disadvantage through treatment they havexperienced; and

(b) Economically disadvantaged because of diminished opportunities that haverecluded these individuals from successfully competing in the open marketplaces evidenced by documentation on the following:

(1) The personal financial statement of the individual(s) seeking economicallyisadvantaged status;

(2) The financial condition of the business enterprise; and

(3) The applicant&#8217;s lack of access to credit and capital as compared tothers in the same line of business.

803.2 803.2 An applicant seeking DBE certification shall provide the Department with narrative describing how the owner or owners of more than 50% of the businessnterests is/are socially and economically disadvantaged as described in thisection. The applicant shall also provide the Department with the personalinancial statement of the owner or owners claiming to be economicallyisadvantaged demonstrating that his or her personal net worth, excluding thealue of his or her primary residence and the value of his or her ownership

interest in the CBE, is less than $1,000,000.
