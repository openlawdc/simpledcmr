§ 30-213. VOLUNTARY SURRENDER OF LOTTERY LICENSE (§ 30-213)

213.1. The agent shall notify the Agency at least thirty (30) days in advance, in writing, of the agent&#8217;s intent or desire to surrender the Lottery license.

213.2. The agent shall allow the Agency or the Agency&#8217;s representative access to the location to remove the on-line computer terminal and return all Agency property including the following:

(a) Agent&#8217;s lottery license;

(b) Agent identification card and stamp;

(c) The on-line computer terminal; and

(d) All other Agency property.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 8665, 8674 (December 29, 1989).

HISTORICAL NOTE

Prior to December 29, 1989, the D.C. Lottery and Charitable Games Control Board published Final Rulemaking published at 29 DCR 2202 (May 28, 1982).
