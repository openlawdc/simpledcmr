§ 17-3805. HEARINGS AND APPEAL (§ 17-3805)

3805.1. Any licensee on whom a notice has been served pursuant to § 3804 may file a written notice of appeal with the Office of Administrative Hearings (OAH).

3805.2. All hearings and appeals shall be conducted pursuant to the regulations promulgated by OAH. Any stay of an OAH decision that results in the revocation of a license shall be issued pursuant to the procedures set forth by OAH.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12137 (November 28, 2008).
