§ 29-7005. APPEAL OF INELIGIBILITY DETERMINATION (§ 29-7005)

7005.1. The applicant may submit a notice of appeal of the ineligibility determination, and any additional documents, records and information that the applicant wishes to have considered, to the Mayor.

7005.2. The notice of appeal, and any additional documents, records and information that the applicant wishes to have considered, shall be submitted within 30 calendar days of receiving the letter described in §7004.2.

7005.3. After considering the notice of appeal and any additional documents, records and information submitted by the applicant, the Mayor shall determine whether, in his or her reasonable judgment, to affirm or reverse the determination that the applicant is not eligible for the tuition assistance.

7005.4. The Mayor shall inform the applicant in writing of the determination made under §7005.3. This determination is final and shall not be subject to further administrative review.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 5013, 5019 (June 1, 2001).
