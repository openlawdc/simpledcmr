§ 11-1571. GENERAL PROVISIONS (CHC) (§ 11-1571)

1571.1. The CHC Overlay District is mapped in combination with, and not instead of, the underlying Commercial District and the Capitol Interest (CAP) Overlay District.

1571.2. All buildings and structures permitted under §§ 1572 and 1573 and the pertinent regulations of the underlying Commercial District and the CAP Overlay District shall be permitted in the combined district.

1571.3. Except as provided in §§ 1572.2 and 1572.4, where there is a conflict between §§ 1572 and 1573 and the underlying Commercial District or the CAP Overlay District, the provisions of §§ 1572 and 1573 shall govern.
