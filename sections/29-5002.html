§ 29-5002. ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS (§ 29-5002)

5002.1. To be eligible to receive PCA services, a Medicaid beneficiary must meet all of the following qualifications:

Be unable to independently perform one or more activities of daily living for which personal care services are needed;

(b) Be in receipt of a written order for PCA services in accordance with Sections 5006.1 and 5006.2; and

(c) Be in receipt of a PCA Service Authorization in accordance with Section 5003.

SOURCE
