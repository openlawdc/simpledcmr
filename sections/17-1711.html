§ 17-1711. ENFORCEMENT; CRIMINAL PENALTIES; PROSECUTION BY THE CORPORATION COUNSEL (§7-1711)

1711.1. An athlete agent that violates § 1710 of this chapter is guilty of a misdemeanor and, upon conviction, is punishable by maximum fine of ten thousand dollars ($10,000) or imprisonment for six (6) months, or both. The Corporation Counsel shall prosecute violations of § 1711 in the name of the District of Columbia.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking at 50 DCR 9549 (November 14, 2003).
