§ 17-6112. ASSESSMENT TOOLS (§ 17-6112)

6112.1. Each program shall develop guidelines to assess whether a program participant:

(a) Has the ability to self-administer his or her medications;

(b) Requires the prescribed medication to be administered by a trainedmedication employee; or

(c) Requires the prescribed medication to be administered by a licensed practical or registered nurse.

6112.2. The program shall monitor each program participant&#8217;s ability to self-medicate as follows:

(a) A registered nurse shall annually review the program participant&#8217;s ability to self-administer medication correctly and document the program participant&#8217;s ability to continue self-medication in the participant&#8217;s records.

(b) A trained medication employee shall review the program participant&#8217;s ability to self-administer medication at least quarterly and document the program participant&#8217;s ability to self-administer medication as prescribed.
