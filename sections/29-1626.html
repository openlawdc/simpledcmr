§ 29-1626. FINALIZATION OF ADOPTION (§ 29-1626)

1626.1. Except in a case of an international adoption, the adoptive family shall file for adoption of a child six (6) months following the placement or as soon thereafter as possible.

1626.2. The child-placing agency shall provide a report to the court summarizing the supervisory period and making a recommendation on the petition for adoption.

1626.3. The family shall provide a copy of the final decree to the child-placing agency.

1626.4. In cases where two (2) child-placing agencies participated in the adoptive placement, the child-placing agency responsible for the finalization of the adoption in court shall forward a copy of the decree to the other participating child-placing agency.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3054 (May 11, 1990).
