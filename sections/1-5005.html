§ 1-5005. DEFINITIONS (§ 1-5005)

5005.1. For the purposes of this chapter, the following words and phrases shall have the meaning ascribed:

Agency- District agency that awards a subgrant.

Grant &#8211; The grant received by a District agency from which the agency makes subgrant awards.

OGMD &#8211; The Office of Grants Management and Development.

Notice of Funding Availability or NOFA &#8211; A public notice that an agency will issue a Request for Applications, informing interested parties when and where the Request for Applications may be obtained.

Request For Applications or RFA &#8211; The document that describes the requirements for subgrant applications.

Review Panel &#8211; A team of qualified individuals responsible for reviewing, scoring and recommending applicants for subgrant award.

Subgrant Award &#8211; The commitment of funds from a grant by an agency to a governmental or private organization to support specific services and operations.

Subgrant Certification Form or SCF &#8211; A certification by an agency that a grant qualifies for subgranting.

Subgrant Officer &#8211; An agency official authorized to execute subgrant awards for the agency.

Subgrant Competitive Review Form or SCRF &#8211; A transmittal form for submitting proposed awards for review and approval by the Office of Grants Management and Development.

Subgrantee &#8211; An organization to which a subgrant has been awarded.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 4517, 4524 (May 21, 1999).
