§ 29-553. EMERGENCY MEDICAL DISPATCH (§ 29-553)

553.1 553.1 The Director may develop the District of Columbia Pre-Hospital Emergency Medical Dispatch Protocol Guidelines for Emergency Medical Dispatch Providers (Pre-Hospital EMD Guidelines).

553.2. The Director may appoint an Emergency Medical Dispatch (EMD) review committee to assist in the development, review, and recommendations of the Pre-Hospital EMD Guidelines.

553.3. The EMS Officer shall review and the Director shall approve the EMD protocols of each Emergency Medical Services Agency prior to their implementation.

553.4 553.4 The Director may issue an emergency protocol or revision which shall have immediate effect if a delay in the issuance of a protocol, protocol revision, or supplemental protocol would pose a threat to the health and welfare of patients.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 16569 (December 6, 2013).
