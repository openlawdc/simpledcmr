§ 1-2975. PUBLIC BENEFITS CASES – HEARING DATES (§ 1-2975)

2975.1 2975.1 After a hearing request is filed, an Administrative Law Judge ordinarilyill schedule a hearing. If any applicable law requires that an administrativeeview be completed before a hearing takes place, a hearing will not becheduled until the administrative review has been completed.

2975.2 2975.2 An Administrative Law Judge may schedule a status conference or otherreliminary hearing in order to simplify the issues in the case, identify thearties’ legal and factual positions, rule on any preliminary legal issues, oror any other purpose.

2975.3 2975.3 Any party may ask an Administrative Law Judge for a different hearingate. Copies of a request form will be sent with every hearing notice and arevailable from OAH.

2975.4 2975.4 Only an Administrative Law Judge can change a hearing date.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 2009, 2013 (March 17, 2006); asmended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 12541, 12588 (December1, 2010).
