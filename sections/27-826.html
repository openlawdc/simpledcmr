§ 27-826. DECISIONS (§ 27-826)

826.1 826.1 The Commission shall issue a decision in writing no later than ninety90) days after a hearing record is closed.

826.2 826.2 A decision of the Commission that is adverse to an applicant orespondent shall contain the following:

(a) Findings of fact;

(b) Conclusions of law;

(c) An order; and

(d) A statement informing the applicant or respondent of the right to have theecision reviewed by the District of Columbia Court of Appeals and the timeithin which a petition for judicial review is required to be submitted by theules of that Court.

826.3 826.3 The Commission shall serve a copy of the decision on the applicant orespondent and the Assistant Attorney General of record in the matter within ten10) days of the date the Commission issues the decision.

826.4 826.4 If a Commission decision is wholly or partially based on official noticef a material fact that is not on the record, the applicant or respondent andhe Assistant Attorney General of record in the matter may oppose the taking offficial notice in a motion for reconsideration submitted to the Commission.

826.5 826.5 The Commission’s decision may modify the period of time which bars aubmission of an application or respondent for certification.

826.6 826.6 A respondent who has been denied the renewal of a certificate or whoseertificate has been revoked shall not be eligible to receive any preferencesor being a CBE or be awarded any SBE set-aside contracts.
