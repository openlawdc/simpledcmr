§ 21-1402. EXEMPTIONS (§ 21-1402)

1402.1. The requirements for obtaining plan approval set forth in §1401.1 shall not apply to any of the following activities:

(a) The harvesting of fish, shellfish or crabs;

(b) Any action requiring approval under §7 of D.C. Law 5-188, D.C. Code §6-926 (1995 Repl. Vol.); or

(c) Recreational boating or angling activities which might incidentally affect submerged aquatic vegetation.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 1971, 1972 (April 15, 1994).
