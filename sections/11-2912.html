§ 11-2912. LOADING (§ 11-2912)

2912.1 2912.1 Loading shall be provided as required in chapter 22 for the C-3-Cistrict.

2912.2 2912.2 The Zoning Commission may, as a special exception, permit the reduction,limination, relocation or consolidation of loading facilities, provided thathe applicant demonstrates that:

The proposed number of loading facilities will be adequate to serve the relatedses;

The loading facilities are designed such that loading and unloading of serviceehicles would be accomplished without the need to move goods and materialscross public areas;

The proposed loading facilities would not tend to affect adversely other modesf transportation using the adjacent public or private transportation way, asefined in § 2900.5;

The proposed loading facilities would not tend to affect adjacent propertiesdversely; and

The Commission may impose conditions as to screening, lighting, coping,etbacks, fences, location of entrances and exits, widening of abuttingransportation ways, or any other requirement it deems necessary to protect thedjacent property and the safety and function of other modes of travel.
