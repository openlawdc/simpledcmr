§ 4-1105. EXEMPTIONS (§ 4-1105)

1105.1. Prospective construction contractors shall be exempt from submitting Affirmative Action Programs for contracts amounting to less than twenty-five thousand dollars ($25,000); provided, that when a construction contractor accumulates contracts amounting to twenty-five thousand dollars ($25,000) or more within a period of twelve (12) months that contractor shall be required to submit an Affirmative Action Program for each contract executed thereafter.

1105.2. Prospective non-construction contractors shall be exempt from submitting Affirmative Action Programs for contracts amounting to less than ten thousand dollars ($10,000); provided, that when a non-construction contractor accumulates contracts amounting to ten thousand dollars ($10,000) or more during a period of twelve (12) months that contractor shall be required to submit an Affirmative Action Program for each contract executed thereafter.
