§ 1-100. GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 1-100)

100.1. The purpose of this chapter is to set forth rules and applicable regulations related to the Office of the Mayor.

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is section 422 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act of 1973, as amended, Pub. L. No. 93-198, 87 Stat. 790 (codified at D.C. Code § 1-242 (1999 Repl.)).

SOURCE

1 DCRR, General Explanation, Special Edition (July 27, 1970).
