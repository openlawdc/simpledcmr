8903.2 8903.2 Good Cause Hearings shall follow the following guidelines:

A participant can only request a Good Cause Hearing if the participant has beenerminated from the Housing Choice Voucher Program.

If the Office of Fair Hearings receives an Informal Hearing request that doesot comply with the deadlines in § 8903.1, the Office of Fair Hearings willotify the participant in writing of the right to request a Good Cause Hearing.

The sole issue for determination in the Good Cause Hearing shall be whether thearticipant had good cause for failing to timely request an Informal Hearing.

In determining whether the participant has demonstrated good cause, the Hearingfficer shall consider the following factors:

The Hearing Officer shall only considervidence regarding the timeliness of the request and the factors listed in §903.2(f) at the Good Cause Hearing.
