§ 29-2703. REIMBURSEMENT FOR PRESCRIPTIONS (§ 29-2703)

2703.1 2703.1 The District of Columbia Medicaid Program shall reimburse claimsubmitted by participating providers for the following prescriptions:

Legend drugs that are prescribed for their labeled use; and

Over-the-counter (OTC) medications as listed in the District Medicaid Preferredrug List and the Pharmacy Billing Manual. The following categories of OTCedications shall be covered when prescribed by a licensed provider:

(1) Oral analgesics with a single active ingredient (such as, aspirin,

acetaminophen, and ibuprofen);

(2) Ferrous salts (such as, sulfate and gluconate);

(3) Antacids with up to three active ingredients (such as, aluminum,

magnesium, and bismuth)

(4) Diabetic preparations (such as, insulin and syringes);

(5) Pediatric, prenatal, and geriatric vitamin formulations;

(6) Family planning drugs and supplies; and

(7) Senna extract, single dose preparations when required for diagnosticadiological procedures performed under the supervision of a physician.
