§ 18-1311. DURATION OF COMMERCIAL DRIVERS LICENSE (§ 18-1311)

1311.1. The commercial driver’s license shall be valid for an eight (8) year period.

1311.2. The Director, or his or her designee, may modify the duration of the commercial driver’s license to comply with any provision of this chapter.

1311.3. Any endorsement issued pursuant to this Chapter shall not expire before the date that the license expires.

SOURCE
