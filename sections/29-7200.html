7200.1. For the purposes of payments under TANF (D.C. Official Code § 205.52), POWER (D.C. Official Code §4-205.78), General Assistance for Children (D.C. Official Code § 4-205.05a) and Interim Disability Assistance (D.C. Official Code § 4-204.07), effective October 1, 2007, the District of Columbia&#8217;s payments levels are adjusted as set forth in § 7200.2.

7200.2. Pursuant to D.C. Official Code §4-205.52(d), the payment levels set forth in this subsection shall apply to public assistance payments made after October 1, 2007.

Pursuant to section 552 of the District of Columbia Public Assistance Act of 1982, effective April 6, 1982 (D.C. Law 4-101; D.C. Official Code § 4-205.52), a TANF recipient who has received TANF benefits for more than 60 months, whether or not consecutive months, shall be eligible to receive no more than the payment levels as set forth in § 7200.4.

Effective February 1, 2011, the payment levels set forth in this subsection shall apply to recipients who have received TANF benefits for more than 60 months:
