§ 1-2921. RENTAL HOUSING CASES – BEGINNING A CASE (§ 1-2921)

2921.1 2921.1 A party may begin a rental housing case by filing a petition with theent Administrator in accordance with 14 DCMR § 3901.

2921.2 2921.2 The timeliness of the filing of any petition shall be measured from theate the Rent Administrator accepts it for filing.

2921.3 2921.3 The Rent Administrator may refuse to accept a petition for filing asrovided in14 DCMR § 3901.

2921.4 2921.4 After filing, the Rent Administrator shall forward the petition and allccompanying papers to OAH, together with a copy of the registration statementor the housing accommodation.

2921.5 2921.5 When OAH receives a petition from the Rent Administrator, OAH shall openhe case. The parties then shall file all papers and attachments at OAH inccordance with Section 2809.
