§ 1-2930. RENTAL HOUSING CASES – HEARINGS (§ 1-2930)

2930.1 2930.1 A petition received by OAH will be treated as a request for a hearing.

OAH will schedule a status conference, a hearing, or mediation. OAH shallotify the parties of the hearing date and of their right to obtain a lawyer ateast fifteen (15) calendar days before a hearing.

2930.2 2930.2 An Administrative Law Judge may dismiss any petition or any claim in aetition without holding a hearing if the Rental Housing Act does not provideelief for the claim(s). The Administrative Law Judge shall first give thearties notice and an opportunity to respond.
