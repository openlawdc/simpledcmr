§ 11-3099. DEFINITIONS (§ 11-3099)

3099.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have the meanings ascribed:

Commission &#8211; the Zoning Commission for the District of Columbia.

Director &#8211; the Director of the Office of Zoning, or such successor official as shall be designated to be the supervisor of the full-time administrative staff of the Commission.

Member &#8211; a member of the Zoning Commission of the District.

Party &#8211; an applicant, or the Advisory Neighborhood Commission for the area within which the property that is the subject of the contested case is located, or any person who is admitted as a party by the Commission pursuant to § 3022. Parties shall have all those rights set forth in this chapter, specifically including §§ 3000, 3004, 3020, 3022, 3024, 3025, and 3029.

Person &#8211; includes an individual, partnership, association, corporation, or public agency.

Presiding officer &#8211; the chairperson or the chairperson pro tem of the Zoning Commission.

SOURCE
