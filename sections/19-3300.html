§ 19-3300. PURPOSE (§ 19-3300)

3300.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish standards and guidelines for the submission of applications for grants to the District of Columbia Sports Commission (the “Commission”).

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is section 7 of D.C. Law 10-152, “the Omnibus Sports Consolidation Act of 1994,” D.C. Code § 2-4006 (1999 Supp.).

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 5506 (September 26, 1997); incorporating by reference the text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 4705 (August 15, 1997).
