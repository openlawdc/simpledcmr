§ 27-809. SCOPE AND TERM OF CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION (§ 27-809)

809.1 809.1 A certificate issued to Certified Business Enterprises shall:

(a) Authorize the CBEs to receive the benefits as outlined in the Act and thishapter on all District government solicitations; and

(b) Be effective for a period of two (2) years from the date of issuance,rovided that the CBE remains in compliance with the Act and these regulations.

809.2 809.2 A CBE that was previously certified but the certification has expiredhall not be eligible for any benefits provided under the Act or theseegulations until such time as the CBE has been recertified.

809.3 809.3 A CBE shall submit an application for certification at least ninety (90)ays prior to the expiration of its registration with the Department. Thepplication shall be signed and sworn to on a form prescribed by the Departmentnd accompanied by documentation and/or other supporting information required byhe Department.
