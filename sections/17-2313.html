§ 17-2313. INACTIVE STATUS (§ 17-2313)

2313.1 2313.1 Upon application by a licensee and payment of the required fee, theoard shall place a licensee on inactive status.

2313.2 2313.2 The Board shall issue a license to an individual who is on inactivetatus and who desires to resume practice as a real estate appraiser if thendividual meets the following requirements:

(a) Pays the required fee;

(b) Demonstrates completion of all continuing education that would have beenequired had the individual been on active status; and

(c) Applies for license renewal within five (5) years of going on inactivetatus.

2313.3 2313.3 The Board may take disciplinary action against a licensee on inactivetatus.

2313.4 2313.4 A licensee may remain on inactive status for a period not to exceed five5) consecutive years.
