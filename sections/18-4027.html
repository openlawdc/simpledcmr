§ 18-4027. SIGHTSEEING LIMOUSINE AND BUS STANDS (§ 18-4027)

4027.1. Sightseeing limousine stands and sightseeing bus stands shall be established at the locations listed in this section.

(a) E Street, N.W.

From a point approximately 39 feet west of 10th Street to a point approximately 109 feet west of 10th Street, on the north side, &#8220;No Parking 9am-7pm, Daily, Old Towne Trolley Only&#8221;.
