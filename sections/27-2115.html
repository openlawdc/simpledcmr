§ 27-2115. PROCUREMENT OF PRINTING AND RELATED GOODS (§ 27-2115)

2115.1. Unless otherwise authorized by law, printing services and related goods for District agencies shall be obtained from the Printing Division, Department of Administrative Services.

2115.2. The contracting officer shall obtain and include in the purchase order or the contract file prior written approval from the Chief of the Printing Division when obtaining printing services and related goods, whether directly or through contracts for other goods or services, from sources other than the Printing Division.

2115.3. When commercial printing services are procured, the contracting officer shall comply with all applicable provisions of this title.
