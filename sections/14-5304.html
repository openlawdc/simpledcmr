§ 14-5304. ALIMONY AND CHILD SUPPORT AS INCOME (§ 14-5304)

5304.1 5304.1 DCHA shall count court-awarded amounts for alimony and child supportnless DCHA verifies that the payments have not been made in the last six (6)onths.

5304.2 5304.2 If the amounts received for child support or alimony are not received on regular basis, DCHA shall anticipate income by performing an average of theast twelve (12) complete months. The average shall include months in which noayments were received and months in which lump sum amounts were received toatch up for prior missed payments.
