§ 24-804. CREDENTIALS (§ 24-804)

804.1. During the inaugural period, no person who is not a member of a duly organized committee regularly appointed for service in connection with the inaugural ceremonies shall wear the badge or insignia of an inaugural committee, or falsely represent himself or herself to be a member of any committee.

804.2. No person shall use, display, or duplicate any press, photographer&#8217;s, radio, television, vehicle, or any other pass issued by the Chief of Police or under authority of this act, unless the person is authorized to do so by the Chief of Police in writing.

SOURCE

Section 3 of the Presidential Inauguration Special Regulations and Rule of Interpretation Concerning Nonrevival of Statutes Act of 1982, D.C. Law 4-125, 29 DCR 2093 (May 21, 1982).
