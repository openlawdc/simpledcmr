§ 27-816. COMPLAINT PROCEDURE (§ 27-816)

816.1 816.1 Any person may file a complaint with the Commission, pursuant to363(b)(1) of the Act, by submitting a completed notarized complaint form,rovided by the Department, to the Department identifying the nature of theomplaint and swearing to the truth of the allegations in the complaint.

816.2 816.2 The Department will forward the complaint to the Commission for review.

The Commission shall take up the complaint at its next scheduled meeting andetermine the action to be taken as outlined in the Act.

816.3 816.3 If the Commission determines that a complaint is not frivolous ortherwise without merit, it shall instruct the Department to investigate theacts surrounding the allegations, including reviewing the file and allaperwork contained therein, interviewing witnesses, or any other reasonablection necessary given the nature of the allegations in the complaint.

816.4 816.4 Within 45 days of the Commission’s determination and instruction tohe Department to investigate, the Department shall provide the results of thenvestigation to the Commission.
