§ 11-1699. DEFINITIONS (CG) (§ 11-1699)

1699.1. When used in this chapter, the term &#8220;residential uses&#8221; shall have the same meaning as the term &#8220;residential purposes&#8221; as set forth in § 631.2, except as otherwise qualified. See § 1601.3 for example.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 72 (January 7, 2005); as corrected by Errata Notice published at 58 DCR 4314, 4315 (May 20, 2011).
