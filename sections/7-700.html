§ 7-700. GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 7-700)

700.1. The District of Columbia&#8217;s In-School Employment Program for Youth is established by §2(a)(2) of the Youth Employment Act of 1979.

700.2. The purpose of this program is to provide school students with subsidized part-time employment at the federal minimum wage level throughout the school year.

700.3. Priority shall be given to economically disadvantaged youth currently in school, but identified as potential dropouts.

700.4. The program shall also provide a basic and meaningful work experience to enhance a youth&#8217;s future employability in the unsubsidized labor market.
