§ 30-1501. RAFFLE WORKERS (§ 30-1501)

1501.1. Raffle workers shall be eighteen (18) years of age or older.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3788, 3811 (May 20, 1988).

HISTORICAL NOTE
