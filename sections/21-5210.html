§ 21-5210. NON-UNION APPEALS (§ 21-5210)

5210.1. This regulation applies only to non-union full-time employees in regular positions who have completed their probationary period.

5210.2. A non-union full-time employee in a regular position who has completed his or her probationary period has the right to appeal a final disciplinary action resulting in a suspension for a period greater than 30 days or termination.

5210.3. All employees may appeal their annual performance rating in accordance with the Authority&#8217;s personnel policies and procedures.
