§ 1-2927. RENTAL HOUSING CASES – SUBSTITUTION OR ADDITION OF PARTIES (§ 1-2927)

2927.1 2927.1 An Administrative Law Judge may substitute or add a party underubsection 2816.1 if: a party dies; a party entity is dissolved or reorganized; party entity’s ownership or interest changes; or an amended registrationtatement for the housing accommodation is filed under 14 DCMR § 4103.

2927.2 2927.2 If a party has been incorrectly named, the Administrative Law Judge mayubstitute or add the correct party under Subsection 2816.1.
