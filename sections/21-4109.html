§ 21-4109. DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA CLEAN RIVERS IMPERVIOUS SURFACE AREA CHARGEINCENTIVEISCOUNT PROGRAM[COLON] ADMINISTRATIVE APPEALS AND JUDICIAL REVIEW (§ 21-4109)

4109.1. Any appeals related to a DDOE action regarding BMP application review or approval, eligibility, and stormwater retention volume calculations shall be taken in accordance with Section 563 of this title.

4109.2. Appeals of DC Water actions regarding the IAC Incentive Discount shall be taken in accordance with Chapter 4 of this title.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11244 (August 2, 2013).
