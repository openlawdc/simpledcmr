§ 29-2603. BENEFITS AVAILABLE FOR BURIAL AND CREMATION (§ 29-2603)

2603.1. When a deceased person meets the eligibility criteria described in section 2602 and an application has been filed and approved as required under section 2601, one (1) of the following shall occur:

(a) Eight hundred dollars ($ 800) shall be paid for burial assistance; or

(b) Four hundred and fifty dollars ($ 450) shall be paid for cremation assistance.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 657, 659 (January 19, 1990); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 6776 (November 8, 1991); as amended by Emergency Rulemaking at 51 DCR 3327 (March 26, 2004) [EXPIRED]; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 5211 (May 21, 2004).
