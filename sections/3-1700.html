§ 3-1700. ALLOCATION OF LOCAL POLITICAL PARTY OFFICIALS BY WARD (§ 3-1700)

1700.1. Each District of Columbia political party shall specify the number and titles of its officers or party committee members to be elected at-large and by ward.

1700.2. The chairperson of the local party committee shall indicate the party’s intention to elect officials or members by written communication signed by the chairperson and filed with the Board no later than one hundred eighty (180) days before the date of the election pursuant to D.C. Code § 1-1001.08(l)(l) (2006 Repl.).

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is section 5 of an Act to regulate the election of delegates representing the District of Columbia to national political conventions and for other purposes (popularly referred to as &#8220;the District of Columbia Election Act of 1955&#8243;), 69 Stat. 700, ch. 862, § 5, D.C. Code § 1-1001.05(a)(14) (2006 Repl.).

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 D.C. Reg. 2006, 2021 (March 11, 1988); and further amended by Final Rulemaking published at 43 D.C. Reg. 1078, 1110 (March1, 1996).

HISTORICAL NOTE

Prior to March 11, 1988, the Board of Elections and Ethics published Final Rulemaking notices at 27 D.C. Reg. 2763 (June 27, 1980), incorporating the text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 27 D.C. Reg. 1929, 1978 (May 9, 1980); and at 29 D.C. Reg. 2798, 2802 (July 2, 1982).
