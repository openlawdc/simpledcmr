§ 29-2553. ADMINISTRATIVE REVIEW NOTICE REQUIREMENTS (§ 29-2553)

2553.1 2553.1 Upon receipt of a request for a fair hearing, the Department’sdministrative Review Office shall schedule an administrative review. As soons possible after receipt of the request for a fair hearing, the Departmenthall mail and, if possible, transmit by facsimile, a notice of thedministrative review to the client, the client representative, if there is one,he Provider, and the Department’s representative if there is a Departmentction at issue.

2553.2 2553.2 The notice shall contain the following information:

(a) The date, time, and place of the review;

(b) The purpose of the review;

(c) That the client has the right to have an attorney or lay representativeresent at the administrative review;

(d) That the client or client representative has the right to submit issues andomments in writing to the Department, prior to or at the time of thedministrative review;

(e) That the client or client representative has the right to review therovider’s or Department’s records regarding the client, or the records ofther related service Providers regarding the client at any time during thedministrative review process;

(f) That the review will not be held unless the client appears and that thelient’s failure to appear will not affect the client’s right to the fairearing previously requested;

(g) That if an administrative review is conducted, the administrative reviewill be completed and a decision issued in writing within fifteen (15) daysfter the receipt by the Department’s Administrative Review Office of theequest for a fair hearing, unless good cause is shown;

(h) That if the client is not satisfied with the result of the administrativeeview, the fair hearing previously requested will be held; and

(i) That if the client is satisfied with the result of the administrativeeview, the client’s request for a fair hearing shall be considered formallyithdrawn upon the submission of a signed statement by the client or clientepresentative to the Office of Administrative Hearings confirming suchithdrawal.
