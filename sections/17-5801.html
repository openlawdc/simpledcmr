§ 17-5801. GENERAL REQUIREMENT (§ 17-5801)

5801.1. Only a person currently licensed as a registered nurse under chapter 54 of this title shall be eligible to apply for a certificate to practice nurse-midwifery under this chapter.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 3497 (May 12, 1989); and amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 11751 (December 27, 2002).
