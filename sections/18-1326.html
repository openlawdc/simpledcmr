§ 18-1326. FEES (§ 18-1326)

1326.1. Every applicant for a commercial driver’s license shall pay a non-refundable fee, payable to the D.C. Treasurer, for the following commercial driver’s license transactions:

Commercial Driver’s Instruction License

First Issuance

$ 78.00

Renewals

$ 78.00

Commercial Driver’s License

Duplicate

$ 20.00

First Issuance

$ 117.00

Renewals

$ 117.00

Reinstatement

$ 98.00

Upgrade or Downgrade after First Issuance

$ 13.00

1326.2. Commercial Driver’s Instruction License shall include all knowledge tests and one (1) skills retest.

1326.3. Commercial Driver’s Instruction License shall be renewable once, after which the holder shall reapply.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 10913 (November 26, 2004); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 850 (February 10, 2006); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 9016, 9017 (October 21, 2011).
