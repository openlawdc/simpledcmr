§ 29-2813. AUTHORIZATION FOR DISINTERMENT AND REINTERMENT (§ 29-2813)

2813.1. An authorization for disinterment and reinterment of a dead body shall be issued by the Registrar upon receipt of an application which meets the requirements of § 2813.2 or upon receipt of an order of a D.C. Court directing the action.

2813.2. An application for disinterment and reinterment shall be:

(a) Signed by the next of kin or legal representative of the deceased and by the person who is in charge of the disinterment; and

(b) Approved by the Director of the Department of Health.

2813.3. The application shall specify the place of disinterment and reinterment.

2813.4. A dead body properly prepared by an embalmer and deposited in a receiving vault shall not be considered a disinterment when removed from the vault for final disposition.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 781, 793 (January 25, 1991); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 1664 (February 23, 2007).
