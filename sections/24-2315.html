§ 24-2315. APPROVAL PERIOD (§ 24-2315)

2315.1 2315.1 The sixty- (60-) day period for issuance of a certificate under § 207f the Act (D.C. Official Code § 7-2502.07(b) (2012 Repl.)) may be extended forood cause in the event that the investigation into the applicant’sualifications has not been completed.

2315.2 2315.2 Reasons that an extension may be granted for good cause shall includehe following:

(a) Non-receipt of the results of an F.B.I. fingerprint check;

(b) Non-receipt of responses from other law enforcement agencies queried abouthe applicant;

(c) Lost, mutilated, or destroyed records requiring reproduction oreplacement; or

(d) A substantial question concerning the applicant’s eligibility thatequires further inquiry.

2315.3 2315.3 Any extension taken shall not exceed thirty (30) calendar days.

2315.4 2315.4 The applicant shall be notified of the extension by letter.

2315.5 2315.5 An application shall be automatically held in abeyance if the applicantas any other certificate pending, or becomes liable to revocation on any otherertificate.

2315.6 2315.6 An application that has been held under § 2315.5 shall be approved orenied in accordance with the time limits set forth in this section, after theermination of the revocation proceeding.

2315.7 2315.7 Except as provided in § 2315.5, any application not expressly approvedr denied within the following periods shall be deemed to be denied for theurpose of appealing to the Director:

(a) Within the sixty- (60-) day period required in the Act, unless the periods extended for good cause shown in accordance with this section; or

(b) At the end of the thirty- (30-) day extension period under this section.
