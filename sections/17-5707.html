§ 17-5707. STANDARDS OF CONDUCT (§ 17-5707)

5707.1. A certified-registered nurse anesthetist shall adhere to the standards set forth in the American Association of Nurse Anesthetists&#8217; (AANA) &#8220;Code of Ethics,&#8221; as they may be amended or republished from time to time.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 3491, 3492 (May 12, 1989); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 11751 (December 27, 2002); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 7228 (July 23, 2004).
