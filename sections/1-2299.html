§ 1-2299. DEFINITIONS (§ 1-2299)

2299.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have the meanings ascribed:

Board &#8211; the Gas Station Advisory Board of the District of Columbia, established in the Retail Service Station Act.

Exemption &#8211; a release from the prohibitions on conversions contained in § 5-301(b) and (c) of the Retail Service Station Act of 1976, D.C. Code § 10-231 (1995 Repl. And 1999 Supp.).

Mayor &#8211; the Mayor of the District of Columbia or his or her designee.

Party &#8211; the applicant and any person who has met the requirements for participating in a hearing.
