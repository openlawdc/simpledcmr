§ 1-2924. RENTAL HOUSING CASES – SERVICE (§ 1-2924)

2924.1 2924.1 Every paper filed at OAH in a rental housing case must be served inccordance with § 904 of the Rental Housing Act, D.C. Official Code §2-3509.04, which allows papers to be served by one of the following methods:

(a) By handing the paper to the person, by leaving it at the person’s place ofusiness with some responsible person in charge, or by leaving it at theerson’s usual place of residence with a person of suitable age andiscretion;

(b) By telegram, when the content of the information or document is given to aelegraph company properly addressed and prepaid;

(c) By mail or deposit with the United States Postal Service properly stampednd addressed; or

(d) By any other means that is in conformity with an order of the Rental Housingommission or OAH in any proceeding.
