§ 29-557. CLINICAL QUALITY ASSURANCE AND IMPROVEMENT REPORTS (§ 29-557)

557.1 557.1 Each Emergency Medical Services Agency shall file an annual report, dueo earlier than January 1 and not later than March 1 of each year, which shalltate the number EMS incidents to which the Emergency Medical Services Agencyesponded to in the prior calendar year and the number of ambulance collisionsor the same time period.

557.2 557.2 Each of the following incidents shall be reported to the District EMSfficer no later than seventy-two (72) hours after discovery:

Unexpected loss of physical or mental function of the patient;

Administration of incorrect medication to the patient, regardless of theutcome;

Administration of an incorrect dose of medication to the patient, regardless ofhe outcome;

Termination of resuscitation in the field;

Pediatric cardiac arrest;

Invocation of a District EMS Comfort Care Order/Do Not Resuscitate Order;

Denial or refusal of transport to or by any patient with a Glasgow Coma Score ofourteen (14) or less at the time of denial or refusal;

An ambulance involved in motor vehicle collision while in service;

Positive results on an EMS provider drug test; and

Any incident that the Director has determined to threaten public safety.
