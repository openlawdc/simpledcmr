§ 29-6261. EDUCATIONAL SERVICES (§ 29-6261)

6261.1. Within seventy-two (72) hours of admission, the contracting entity and, where applicable, the CSSD shall provide the facility with the information and documentation necessary for the facility to arrange for and ensure that each school-aged resident attends school or an educational program in accordance with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and the resident&#8217;s initial ISP. Within forty-eight (48) hours of receiving such information from the contracting entity or the CSSD, the facility shall arrange for and ensure that each school-aged resident attends school or an educational program in accordance with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and the resident&#8217;s initial ISP. Such school or educational program shall comply with all applicable laws, standards, and licensing requirements.

6261.2. The facility shall encourage residents to participate in community, school, recreational, and cultural activities as appropriate to the resident&#8217;s age and level of development. To the extent practicable, the facility actively shall support and facilitate participation in and transportation to such activities.

6261.3. The facility shall maintain the resident&#8217;s educational records, including, but not limited to, report cards and transcripts, in the resident&#8217;s case record.
