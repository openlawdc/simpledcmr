1331.2. The civil fines set forth in this section shall be doubled for the second violation of the same infraction, and shall be doubled once more for any subsequent violation or violations of the same infraction

1331.3. The Office of Administrative Hearings shall conduct hearings for violations of infractions delineated in Chapter 13.

In addition to the civil fine, failure to pay the fine or request a hearing within fifteen (15) calendar days of the issuance of a notice of infraction may result in the imposition of a penalty equal to the amount of the civil fine.

1331.3. 1 Failure to appear for a requested hearing may result in the imposition of a penalty equal to twice the amount of the civil fine.
