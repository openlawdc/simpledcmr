§ 27-3707. TERMINATION OF SUBCONTRACTS (§ 27-3707)

3707.1. Upon termination of a prime contract, the prime contractor and each subcontractor shall be responsible for the prompt settlement with their immediate subcontractors. A subcontractor shall have no contractual rights against the District upon termination of the prime contract.

3707.2. Prime contractors shall settle with subcontractors in general conformity with the policies relating to settlement of prime contracts as specified in §§3703 &#8211; 3706 of this chapter.

3707.3. The failure of a prime contractor to include an appropriate termination clause in any subcontract or the failure of the contractor to exercise the rights of the termination clause shall not affect the District&#8217;s right to require the termination of the subcontract, and shall not increase the obligation of the District in any way whatsoever.

3707.4. In no event shall the District be required to pay the prime contractor any amount for loss of anticipatory profits or consequential damages resulting from the termination of any subcontract.
