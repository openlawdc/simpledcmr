§ 23-207. LICENSURE PERIODS (§ 23-207)

207.1. Each license issued by the Board shall be valid for three (3) years, except in the following circumstances:

(a) When suspended or revoked;

(b) In the case of Temporary licenses;

(c) When the license takes effect on a date in between the dates established by the Board for the regular licensure period of each license class, in which case the license shall be valid only until the end of the licensure period; and

(d) In the case of Stipulated licenses.

207.2. The three year renewal period for each license listed below shall occur sequentially every three years starting with the following dates:
