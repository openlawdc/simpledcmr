§ 21-4102. CUSTOMER ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (§ 21-4102)

4102.1 4102.1 CUSTOMER ASSISTANCE PROGRAM FOR HOUSEHOLDS AND TENANTS

(a) Eligible households and tenants will receive an exemption from waterervice charges, sewer service charges, and Payment in Lieu of Taxes (PILOT) andight of Way (ROW) fees for the first Four Hundred Cubic Feet (4 CCF) per monthf water used. If the customer uses less than Four Hundred Cubic Feet (4 CCF)f water in any month, the exemption will apply to that month’s actual watersage.

(b) Participation in the Customer Assistance Program is limited to single-familyesidential accounts and individually metered tenant accounts when the eligiblepplicant is responsible for paying for water services.

(c) Eligibility is determined by the District of Columbia Department ofnvironment Energy Office.
