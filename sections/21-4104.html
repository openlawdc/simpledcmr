§ 21-4104. CUSTOMER CLASSIFICATIONS FOR WATER AND SEWER RATES (§ 21-4104)

4104.1 4104.1 The customer classifications for water and sewer rates shall consist of residential class, multi-family, and a non-residential class:

(a) Residential – a single-family dwelling used for domestic purposes; aondominium or apartment unit where each unit is served by a separate serviceine and is individually metered and the unit is used for domestic purposes; or multifamily structure of less than four apartment units where all the unitsre served by a single service line that is master metered.

(b) Multi-Family – a multifamily structure (such as a condominium orpartment dwelling) used for domestic purposes, with four or more units.

(c) Non-residential – all customers not within either the residential orulti-family class.
