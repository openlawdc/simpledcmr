§ 27-499. DEFINITIONS (§ 27-499)

499.1. E Filing-Electronic transmission of an original document (pleading) to the Board via the Vendor&#8217;s system. An E File consists of a document, an image, or both.

499.2. E Service-Electronic transmission of an original document (pleading) to all other designated recipients via the Vendor&#8217;s system. Upon the completion of any transmission to the Vendor&#8217;s system, a certified receipt is issued to the sender acknowledging receipt by the Vendor system.

499.3. E Document-An electronic file of a word processing document that contains almost exclusively text.

499.4. E Image-An electronic file of a document that has been scanned or converted to a graphical or image format.
