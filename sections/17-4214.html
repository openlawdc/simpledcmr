§ 17-4214. SUPERVISION OF DENTAL HYGIENISTS (§ 17-4214)

4214.1. Except as provided by § 4214.2, a dentist shall not supervise more than two (2) dental hygienists at the same time.

4214.2. Pursuant to § 201(f) of the Act, D.C. Official Code § 3-1202.01(f)(2001), the limitation under this section shall not apply to a dentist who is an employee of, or operating pursuant to a contract with, the District or federal government and/or who is supervising dental hygienists who are employed by or operating pursuant to a contract with the District or federal government.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 927, 935 (February 12, 1988); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 3457 (April 20, 2007).
