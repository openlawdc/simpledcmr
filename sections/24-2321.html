§ 24-2321. QUALIFICATIONS AND PROCEDURES TO OBTAIN A FIREARMS DEALER’S LICENSE (§4-2321)

2321.1 2321.1 A person is eligible to become a licensed dealer of firearms if thaterson:

(a) Is eligible to register a firearm under this chapter;

(b) Is eligible under federal law to engage in such business; and

(c) Has not previously violated any statutory duty of a licensed dealer if thaterson earlier was a licensed dealer.

2321.2 2321.2 The license issued to a firearms dealer shall be valid for a period ofot more than one (1) year from the date of issuance.

2321.3 2321.3 To deal firearms lawfully, the holder of a firearms dealer’s licenseust also comply with any other license or zoning procedures required by law,ncluding having a certificate of occupancy and a basic business license issuedy the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs in accordance withpplicable provisions in the District of Columbia Municipal Regulations.

2321.4 2321.4 Prior to applying to the Firearms Registration Section for a firearmealer’s license, an applicant must first obtain a Federal Firearms Dealer’sicense issued by the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, and Firearms.

2321.5 2321.5 Each application for a dealer’s license and renewal shall be made on aorm prescribed by the Chief, shall be sworn to or affirmed by the applicant,nd shall contain:

(a) All information required by § 203 of the Act (D.C. Official Code §-2502.03 (2013 Supp.));

(b) The address where the applicant conducts or intends to conduct his/herusiness;

(c) Whether the applicant, prior to September 24, 1976, held a license to dealn deadly weapons in the District; and

(d) Such other information as the Chief may require including, but not limitedo, fingerprints and photographs of the applicant.
