§ 19-4335. ESTIMATE OF CONSTRUCTION COSTS (§ 19-4335)

4335.1. An estimate of construction costs shall be prepared by the Library&#8217;s program staff for each proposed contract and for each proposed contract modification estimated to exceed twenty-five thousand dollars ($25,000).

4335.2. The estimate shall be prepared in as much detail as though the Library was competing for award.

4335.3. Access to information concerning the Library estimate shall be limited to Library personnel whose official duties require knowledge of the estimate.

4335.4. The amount of the Library estimate shall not be disclosed, except as otherwise permitted by these regulations.

4335.5. Contracts for construction shall not be awarded at cost to the Library.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 493 (January 18, 2008).
