§ 17-8712. CONTINUING EDUCATION CREDITS (§ 17-8712)

8712.1 8712.1 The Board may grant continuing education credit for whole hours only,ith a minimum of fifty (50) minutes constituting one (1) credit hour.

8712.2 8712.2 The Board may grant a maximum of five (5) hours of continuing educationredits per year to an applicant for attending in-service education programs.

Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 11937, 11949 (December7, 2010).
