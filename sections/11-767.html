§ 11-767. CLOSED COURTS CRITERIA (C-5 (PAD)) (§ 11-767)

767.1. Where a closed court in the C-5 (PAD) District is enlarged for the purpose of obtaining an award of a bonus incentive, the criteria in this section shall be met.

767.2. A closed court shall be landscaped and may be used for a restaurant or a recreational exhibit purpose.

767.3. No permanent structure shall be permitted in the closed court, except structures incidental to the landscaping, such as fountains or art objects.

767.4. Temporary structures incidental to a cafe or an exhibit purpose shall be permitted.

767.5. A closed court for which a bonus incentive award is sought shall be directly accessible to the public and connected to the public space of the main floor of the building.

767.6. Vehicular access or use of the closed court shall be prohibited if a bonus incentive is awarded.

767.7. Only that portion of the area of a closed court in excess of the requirement under § 776 shall qualify for a bonus incentive as provided under § 762.4.

SOURCE
