§ 4-121. APPEALS (§ 4-121)

121.1. An appeal from the final decision of OHR may be taken to the Superior Court of the District of Columbia.

121.2. Pursuant to § 109 and § 113, the final decisions of the Director include a summary determination or the final determination after the hearing examiner’s recommendation.

121.3. The party must file a Petition for Review with the Clerk of the Civil Division within thirty (30) days after service of notice of the final decision.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 31 DCR 56, 79 (January 6, 1984); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 43 DCR 6569, 6576 (December 13, 1996).
