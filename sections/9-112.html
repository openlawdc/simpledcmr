§ 9-112. OPTIONAL TAX COMPUTATION FOR D.C. RESIDENTS (§ 9-112)

112.1. Any resident other than a fiduciary, may elect to compute the tax due in accordance with the Optional Tax Table(s) if taxable income is less than fifty thousand dollars ($ 50,000).

112.2. [Deleted] 30 DCR 1255, 1256 (March 18, 1983).
