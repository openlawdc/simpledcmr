§ 3-909. APPOINTMENT PENDING SPECIAL ELECTION[COLON] NON-PARTY AFFILIATED AT-LARGEOUNCIL SEAT (§ 3-909)

909.1. Within a reasonable period of time after receiving notice from the D.C. Board of Elections and Ethics of a vacancy in a non-party affiliated at-large seat, the Council of the District of Columbia shall appoint a qualified elector who is not affiliated with any political party.

909.2. The elector appointed Councilmember at-large shall fill the office until the D.C. Board of Elections and Ethics holds a special election and certifies the winner, as provided by D.C. Official Code § 1-204.01(d)(2) (2006 Repl.).

909.3. The elector appointed to the Council pursuant to the Charter and this chapter shall, within thirty (30) days of the appointment, comply with the requirements of D.C. Official Code § 1-1106.02(a) and (b) (2006 Repl. &#038; Supp. 2011).

Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 8058, 8064 (September 16, 2011).
