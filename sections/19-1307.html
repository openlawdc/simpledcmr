§ 19-1307. FORTUNE TELLERS, MEDIUMS, CLAIRVOYANTS, SOOTHSAYERS, AND PALM READERS [DELETED]§ 19-1307)

EDITOR’S NOTE

Subsection 2003(pp)(36) of D.C. Law 12-261, the “Secondmnibus Regulatory Reform Amendment Act of 1998” repealed paragraph 43 ofection 7 of An Act Making appropriations to provide for the government of theistrict of Columbia for the fiscal year ending June thirtieth, nineteen hundrednd three, and for other purpose, approved July 1, 1902, which set forth theeneral licensing law provisions governing fortune tellers, mediums,lairvoyants, soothsayers and palm readers.
