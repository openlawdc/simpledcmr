§ 29-3201. STANDARDS FOR NURSE AIDE TRAINING PROGRAMS (§ 29-3201)

3201.1. Sections 3201-3212 establish criteria and standards for nurse aide training and competency evaluation programs that provide training for nurse aides employed in nursing facilities in the District of Columbia.

3201.2. These standards shall:

(a) Ensure that all persons employed as nurse aides have the competencies to perform the nursing duties in a safe and effective manner;

(b) Serve as a guide for the development of nurse aide training programs; and

(c) Provide criteria for the evaluation of new and established nurse aide training programs.

3201.3. Each institution authorized to provide nurse aide training shall obtain a certificate of approval from LTCA.

SOURCE
