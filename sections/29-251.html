§ 29-251. SETTLEMENT (§ 29-251)

251.1. The hearing examiner shall encourage settlement at every stage of the proceeding, if the settlement is consistent with the provisions and objectives of the Act.

251.2. The parties shall submit any settlement agreement to the hearing examiner in writing or orally on the record.

251.3. If the parties agree to settle the case, the hearing examiner shall issue an order setting forth the terms of the settlement and closing the case.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
