§ 29-1601. INCORPORATION (§ 29-1601)

1601.1. Each child-placing agency shall be:

(a) A nonprofit corporation incorporated in the District of Columbia; or

(b) A nonprofit corporation incorporated outside the District of Columbia with a certificate of authority to conduct its affairs in the District of Columbia as a nonprofit corporation.

1601.2. Each child-placing agency shall operate according to by-laws and articles of incorporation which state the purpose of the organization includes the operation of a child-placing agency.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3034 (May 11, 1990); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 59 (January 3, 1997).
