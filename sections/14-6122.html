§ 14-6122. GRIEVANCE PROCEDURES (§ 14-6122)

6122.1. Any applicant seeking admission to public housing operated by DCHA who believes that he or she has been aggrieved, or adversely affected by an act or failure to act by a DCHA employee or official, may file, with the DCHA, a complaint requesting an administrative determination of his or her rights as provided for in 14 DCMR Chapter 63. Applicants seeking admission to the Housing Choice Voucher Program housing may file a complaint as provided in 14 DCMR Chapter 89.
