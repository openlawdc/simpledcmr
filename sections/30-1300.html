§ 30-1300. LIST REQUIRED TO BE MAINTAINED (§ 30-1300)

1300.1. Each licensed organization shall maintain and keep a current list of membership available for inspection at all reasonable times.

1300.2. Upon request of the Board, a licensed organization shall file lists of its current members, officers, and directors and supplementary lists of additions, deletions, and other changes required to keep the original list up-to-date.

AUTHORITY

Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is §4 of the Law to Legalize Lotteries, Daily Numbers Games, and Bingo and Raffles for Charitable Purposes in the District of Columbia, D.C. Law 3-172, D.C. Official Code §3-1301 et seq.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3788, 3801 (May 20, 1988).
