§ 18-4023. PARKING METER ZONES (§ 18-4023)

4023.1. Thirty (30) minute parking meter zones are established on the streets or portions of streets listed in this subsection.

(a) G Street, N.W.

From 9th Street to 10th Street, on both sides, Two Hour Parking, 7:00am-9:30pm, Monday-Saturday.

(a) 14th Street N.W.

From Harvard Street to Columbia Road, on the west side, “One Hour Parking, 7:00a.m.-8:30p.m., Monday-Saturday.”;

From Irving Street to Columbia Road, on the west side, “30-Minute Parking, 7:00a.m.-8:30p.m., Monday-Saturday.”;

From Park Road to Irving Street, on the west side, “30-Minute Parking, 7:00a.m.-8:30p.m., Monday-Saturday.”;

From Park Road to Monroe Street, on the east side, “One Hour Parking, 7:00a.m.-8:30p.m., Monday-Saturday.”;

From Monroe Street to Park Road, on the west side, “One Hour Parking, 9:30a.m.-4:00p.m., Monday-Saturday.”;

4023.4. Three (3) hour parking meter zones are established on the streets or portions of streets listed in this subsection.

4023.5. Three (3) hour Motorcycle Parking Meter Zones are established on the streets or portions of street listed in this subsection and they are established for the days and hours specified for each location. No person shall park any vehicle other than a motorcycle, motor-driven cycle, motor scooter, or motorized bicycle during the days and hours specified that the location is designated as a motorcycle parking meter zone.

4023.6. The parking meter zones established under this section shall be designated by parking meters and marked parking spaces.

4023.7. Restrictions on parking in zones established under this section are in addition to other restrictions which may be applicable under the provisions of this title.

(a) New York Avenue, N.W.

From a point approximately 138 feet west of 5th Street to a point approximately 209 feet west of 5th Street, on the north side, &#8220;Twenty Minute Parking, at all other times.
