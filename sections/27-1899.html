§ 27-1899. DEFINITIONS (§ 27-1899)

1899.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have the meanings ascribed:

Blanket purchase agreement &#8211; a pre-contractual agreement with a vendor that establishes a charge account for goods or services which allows an agency to make small purchases without the issuance of a purchase order for each individual purchase. Payments are made on a monthly basis.

Director &#8211; the Director of the Office of Contracting and Procurement (OCP) or the District of Columbia Chief Procurement Officer (CPO).

Purchase order &#8211; an offer by the District to buy certain goods or services from commercial sources upon specified terms and conditions.

Quotation &#8211; a citation of price and delivery terms or a period of performance by a contractor in response to a contracting officer&#8217;s request on procurements of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) or less.

Small purchase &#8211; a procurement of goods or services in an aggregate amount not exceeding the small purchase authority limitations set forth in this chapter.

Unpriced purchase order &#8211; an order for goods or services, the price of which is not established at the time of issuance of the order.
