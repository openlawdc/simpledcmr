§ 17-4799. DEFINITIONS (§ 17-4799)

4799.1. As used in this chapter, the following terms shall have the meanings ascribed:

Academic year &#8211; an equivalent to four hundred and fifty (450) hours of classroom instruction over two (2) semesters of approximately fifteen (15) weeks or three (3) semesters of approximately ten (10) weeks.

Applicant &#8211; a person applying for a license to practice acupuncture under this chapter.

Board &#8211; the Board of Medicine, established by § 203(a) of the Act, D.C. Code § 2-3302.3 (a) (1988).

Committee &#8211; the Advisory Committee on Acupuncture, established by § 203(b) of the Act, D.C. Code § 2-3302.3(b) (1988).

Contact hour &#8211; sixty (60) minutes spent by an apprentice under the direct supervision of a preceptor who is either discussing or observing the apprentice&#8217;s practice.

Physician &#8211; a person licensed to practice medicine under the Act, including an osteopath.

4799.2. The definitions in § 4099 of chapter 40 of this title are incorporated by reference into and are applicable to this chapter.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 7159, 7167 (October 13, 1989).
