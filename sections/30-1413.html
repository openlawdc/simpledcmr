§ 30-1413. FINANCIAL REPORT (§ 30-1413)

1413.1. A licensed organization shall complete and file a financial report on a form provided by the Board within ten (10) days of a regular bingo operation and within thirty (30) days of a limited-period bingo operation.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3788, 3810 (May 20, 1988).

HISTORICAL NOTE

Prior to May 20, 1988, the D.C. Lottery and Charitable Games Control Board published Final Rulemaking at 29 DCR 5016, 5028 (November 12, 1982).
