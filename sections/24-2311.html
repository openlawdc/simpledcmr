§ 24-2311. KNOWLEDGE OF FIREARMS AND TRAINING REQUIREMENTS (§ 24-2311)

2311.1 2311.1 Knowledge of the laws of the District pertaining to firearms, andnowledge of the safe and responsible use of firearms, shall be tested through aritten examination.

2311.2 2311.2 Under compelling circumstances, an oral test may be administered inlace of the written test.

2311.3 2311.3 The type of test and its content shall be at the sole discretion of theirector.

2311.4 2311.4 [RESERVED].

2311.5 2311.5 [RESERVED].

2311.6 2311.6 Rifles and shotguns shall be considered the same type of firearm for theurposes of testing.

2311.7 2311.7 If an applicant fails an examination, he or she shall be allowed one (1)etest without charge.

2311.8 2311.8 A fee equal to that submitted with the original application may, at theiscretion of the Director, be assessed for the second retest and for eachubsequent retest.

2311.9 2311.9 An applicant shall complete a firearms training and safety classrovided by the Chief or submit evidence of compliance with § 203(a)(13)(B) ofhe Act.
