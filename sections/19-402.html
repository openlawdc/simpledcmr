§ 19-402. BOOKING OF EVENTS BY THE AUTHORITY (§ 19-402)

402.1 402.1 The Authority may book a non-convention event on its own behalf up towenty-four (24) months before the date of the event without prior notice toestination DC. The Authority may not, however, book a non-convention event toe held at the Convention Center more than twenty-four months before the date ofhe event without first providing notice to, and consulting with, DestinationC.

402.2 402.2 To ensure that convention event reservations for events to be held in theonvention Center are given first priority, the Authority shall not assign aate to a non-convention event to be held in the Convention Center if, prior tohe booking of that event, the Authority has received written notice fromestination DC that it is in active negotiations to reserve that date for aonvention event.
