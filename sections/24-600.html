§ 24-600. ADMINISTRATION AND ENFORCEMENT (§ 24-600)

600.1 600.1 No person, firm, corporation, co-partnership, association, trustee, ordministrator shall engage in any of the businesses designated in this chapterithout first having obtained the appropriate license or licenses under thishapter.

600.2 600.2 All licenses shall be issued by the Director of the Department ofonsumer and Regulatory Affairs (Director) or the Director’s designee.

600.3 600.3 Any license issued under this chapter may be suspended or revoked by theirector for failure of the licensee to comply with the laws or regulationspplicable to the licensed business under this chapter.

600.4 600.4 Any violation of any of the provisions of this chapter shall subject theffender to the penalties prescribed in D.C. Official Code § 47-2846 (2001) anditle 16, Chapter 32 of the District of Columbia Municipal Regulations.

600.5 600.5 Any person who makes any false or misleading statement in the filing ofny information required under this chapter shall be subject to the penaltiesrescribed in D.C. Code § 47-2846 (2001) and may have his or her licenseuspended or revoked by the Director.

AUTHORITY

D.C. Official Code §§ 47-2832.01, 47-2851.12, and 47-2851.20.
