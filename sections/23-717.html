§ 23-717. CORKING FEE (§ 23-717)

717.1 717.1 The holder of an on-premises retailer&#8217;s license may permit a patrono bring to and consume on the licensed premises an alcoholic beverage that theicensee is permitted to sell or serve under its on-premises retailer&#8217;sicense; provided that the alcoholic beverage is opened by an employee of thestablishment, However, the holder of an on-premises retailer&#8217;s licensehall not permit any alcoholic beverage opened on the licensed premises to beemoved from the licensed premises.

717.2 717.2 The holder of an on-premises retailer’s license shall be permitted toharge a corking fee provided that the corking fee is disclosed to the patronrior to the opening of the alcoholic beverage.
