§ 3-4105. EXPLORATORY COMMITTEE CONTRIBUTION LIMITATIONS (§ 3-4105)

4105.1. Contributions in support of an exploratory committee shall be received or made in accordance with § 3009 of Chapter 30 of this title, except that individual and aggregate contributions shall be limited for the following exploratory committees to the amounts specified:

(a) Mayor &#8211; $2,000 individual, and $200,000 aggregate;

(b) Chairman of the Council &#8211; $1,500 individual, and $150,000 aggregate;

(c) At-large member of the Council &#8211; $1,000 individual, and $100,000 aggregate;

(d) Ward Councilmember or President of the State Board of Education &#8211; $500 individual, and $50,000 aggregate; and

(e) Member of the State Board of Education &#8211; $200 individual, and $20,000 aggregate.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 1402 (February 8, 2013); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11864 (August 16, 2013).
