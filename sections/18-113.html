§ 18-113. THIRD PARTY TESTING (§ 18-113)

113.1 113.1     The Director may authorize a District of Columbia licensed drivingnstructor (“third party examiner”) employed by a District of Columbiaicensed driving school as described in Chapter 9 of this title (“third partymployer”) to administer the road test described in Section 104 of thishapter, if the following conditions are met:

(a) The road tests given by the third party examiner are the same as those whichould otherwise be given by the Department of Motor Vehicles;

(b) The third party examiner has successfully completed a road test examinerraining course, as approved by the Director;

(c)         The third party employer has an agreement with the Departmentf Motor Vehicles containing, at a minimum, provisions that:

(1)         Allow the Department of Motor Vehicles to conduct randomxaminations of the third party examiner and audits of the employer withoutrior notice;

(2)         Require that all third party examiners meet the sameualification and training standards as Department of Motor Vehicle examiners,o the extent necessary to conduct road tests;

(3) Require that all third party examiners who will administer the skillsest pass an examination to the satisfaction of the Director;

(4) Require that its third party examiners initiate and complete each road testt a Department of Motor Vehicles facility designated by the Director;

(5) Require that the results from each road test administered by a third partyxaminer be delivered to the Department of Motor Vehicles by the examinermmediately after conclusion of the examination;

(6) Require that the third party employer maintain bodily injury and propertyamage liability insurance as set forth by the Director;

(7) Require that the third party employer indemnify and hold harmless theistrict of Columbia and its employees from and against all claims, demands,udgments, losses, damages, and costs arising directly or indirectly from anyct or omission of the third party examiner or third party employer relating tois, her, or its activities under this section.

(8) Allow Department of Motor Vehicles employees to take the tests actuallydministered by the third party examiner as if the Department of Motorehicles’ employee were a test applicant and allow the Department of Motorehicles to test a sample of drivers who were examined by the third partyxaminer to compare pass/fail results;

(9) Allow the third party employer to charge a fee, the maximum of which will beetermined by the Director; and

(10) Reserve to the Department of Motor Vehicles the right to take prompt andppropriate remedial action against the third party employer or the third partyxaminer if either fails to comply with a provision of this section, theepartment of Motor Vehicles standards for the driver’s license testingrogram, or any term of the agreement required by this paragraph.
