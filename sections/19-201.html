§ 19-201. RECRUITING AND HIRING (§ 19-201)

201.1 201.1 The President and Chief Executive Officer shall be responsible forecruiting and hiring for vacant or new positions, in consultation withppropriate Division Directors.

201.2 201.2 The President and Chief Executive Officer shall post vacancynnouncements for regular full-time and regular part-time positions at theuthority, except for the President and Chief Executive Officer and Executivetaff positions, for a minimum of five (5) working days before any job offersay be extended.

201.3 201.3 The following information shall be included in the advertisement for aosition vacancy:

(a) Position title;

(b) Brief description of the work to be performed;

(c) Minimum desirable skills and experience required;

(d) Salary information;

(e) Information required to be submitted with application; and

(f) Equal employment opportunity language as required.

201.4 201.4 Current employees of the Authority shall be eligible to apply fordvertised vacant positions.

201.5 201.5 The President and Chief Executive Officer shall use criteria specificallyelated to the requirements for the position in evaluating an applicant.

201.6 201.6 The President and Chief Executive Officer may authorize reimbursement of prospective employee for interviewing expenses.
