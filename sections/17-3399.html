§ 17-3399. DEFINITIONS (§ 17-3399)

3399.1. As used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases have the meanings ascribed:

Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) &#8211; a hearing examiner authorized to hear cases pursuant to the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs Civil Infractions Act of 1985, D.C. Law 6-42, D.C. Code §§ 6-2701 to 6-2723 (1987 Supp.), or designated by the Director to hear a case.

Applicant &#8211; an applicant for a license under an Act listed in § 3300.1 or rules promulgated pursuant thereto.

Board &#8211; a board or commission listed in § 3300.1.

Business day &#8211; a day other than a Saturday, Sunday, legal holiday, or day on which the Department is officially closed.

Certificate &#8211; a certificate issued by a board pursuant to an Act listed in § 3300.1.

Chairperson &#8211; the chairperson of a board or commission listed in § 3300.1 or a person designated by a board to preside at a hearing or act in place of the chairperson.

Day &#8211; a calendar day.

Department &#8211; the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs.

Director &#8211; the Director of the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs, or the Director&#8217;s designee.

Legal holiday &#8211; one of the following holidays:

(a) New Year&#8217;s Day;

(b) Martin Luther King, Jr.&#8217;s, Birthday;

(c) Washington&#8217;s Birthday;

(d) Memorial Day;

(e) Independence Day;

(f) Labor Day;

(g) Columbus Day;

(h) Veterans Day;

(i) Thanksgiving Day;

(j) Christmas Day; or

(k) Any other day designated as a legal holiday by the President, the Congress, or the Mayor or the Council of the District of Columbia, on the actual day the legal holiday is celebrated by the government of the District of Columbia.

License &#8211; a license issued by a board pursuant to an Act listed in § 3300.1.

Party &#8211; a respondent, the Corporation Counsel, the Director, or any other person recognized by a board as a party in a particular proceeding.

Practitioner &#8211; a person who holds a license issued by a board or commission listed in § 3300.1.

Registration &#8211; a registration issued by a board pursuant to an Act listed in § 3300.1.

Respondent &#8211; a person against whom an adverse action is contemplated, proposed, or taken.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3488, 3516 (May 13, 1988).
