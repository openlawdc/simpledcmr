§ 15-1308. MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS (§ 15-1308)

1308.1. Nothing contained in this chapter shall limit or alter the Commission&#8217;s authority under D.C. Official Code § 34-912(a) (2010 Repl. &#038; 2011 Supp.) to require reimbursement of the expenses, including the expenses of the Office of the People&#8217;s Counsel, of any investigation, valuation, revaluation, or other proceeding by the Commission concerning a particular public utility.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 29 DCR 3245, 3248 (July 30, 1982); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 4230, 4232 (May 4, 2012).
