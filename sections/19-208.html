§ 19-208. ANNUAL LEAVE AND HOLIDAYS (§ 19-208)

208.1 208.1 Regular full-time and regular part-time employees shall accrue annualeave from the first pay period of employment. An employee may take annual leavenly after the first ninety (90) days.

208.2 208.2 An employee shall not take annual leave without obtaining the priorpproval of the employee&#8217;s supervisor. The supervisor may refuse leave ifhe employee&#8217;s absence would interfere with the functioning of theepartment or the Authority.

208.3 208.3 Supervisors shall not advance unearned annual leave to employees, exceptn emergency situations and with the approval of the President and Chiefxecutive Officer.

208.4 208.4 No employee may carry forward more than two hundred forty (240) hours inis or her annual leave account into a new calendar year, without the priorritten approval of the President and Chief Executive Officer.

208.5 208.5 The President and Chief Executive Officer shall determine holidaychedules, with due consideration for the unique nature of the Authority&#8217;susiness and the special needs of its clients.

208.6 208.6 Regular full-time and regular part-time employees shall be eligible toeceive a number of personal holidays determined by the President and Chiefxecutive Officer, in addition to official holidays of the Authority.
