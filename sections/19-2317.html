§ 19-2317. SWEEPS AND FOLLOW-UP TECHNIQUES (§ 19-2317)

2317.1. A contestant shall execute a sweep only by making a sweeping motion with the padded area of the kicking foot, such that only the padded area could strike the opponent.

2317.2. After a successful sweep, a contestant shall not execute any further technique against an opponent if any part of the opponent’s body, except the feet, is touching the floor.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking at 35 DCR 3759, 3765 (May 20, 1988).
