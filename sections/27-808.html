§ 27-808. UPGRADE (§ 27-808)

808.1 808.1 An applicant that has been certified by the Department as a CBE in oner more areas may expand the areas in which it is certified by submitting to theepartment a completed application for an upgrade. Each application for anpgrade shall comply with the requirements of this chapter and shall beccompanied by documentation indicating the applicant&#8217;s qualifications,

expertise, and/or resources which justify the upgrade.

(a) With respect to each category of construction and non-construction servicesor which an applicant seeks certification, the applicant shall demonstrate thathe applicant has the expertise, personnel, facilities, equipment, andxperience to perform the services on an on-going basis.

(b) With respect to SBEs as suppliers of goods and equipment, the applicanthall submit evidence that the applicant is a manufacturer of or regular dealern such goods.

(1) In order to qualify as a manufacturer, an applicant shall own or lease on aegular basis an establishment that produces on the premises the materials,upplies, articles or equipment of the character in which the applicant claimso be a manufacturer; but the term does not include a business which performsnly minimal operations on or minimal assembly of the items being produced.

(2) In order to qualify as a regular dealer under this section, an applicanthall meet the following minimum eligibility requirements:

(A) Maintain an establishment in which materials, supplies, articles, orquipment of the character in which the applicant claims to be a regular dealerre bought, kept in stock, and sold to customers on a recurring basis in thesual course of business; and

(B) Demonstrate that its business is an established and on-going entityegularly dealing in the particular materials, supplies, articles, or equipmentf the character offered in the District of Columbia.

(3) In making the determination whether the applicant is a regular dealer, theepartment shall consider the following factors:

(A) Whether the applicant has performed the particular services for a client inhe course of its business in the one-year period prior to the application date;

(B) If the applicant is newly providing this type of service, whether thepplicant has made plans and committed resources to performing the particularervices for clients; or

(C) The customary practice in the industry.
