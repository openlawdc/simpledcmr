§ 14-9811. MOVE-IN AND MOVE-OUT INSPECTIONS (§ 14-9811)

9811.1 9811.1 Prior to the move-in date, DCHA shall conduct a move-in inspectionith the AYBL Family. At the end of the inspection, DCHA and the AYBL Familyhall sign the unit inspection form certifying the condition of the unit, andhe equipment provided with the unit. The AYBL Family cannot contest anyeficiency not included on the move-in inspection form.

9811.2 9811.2 AYBL Families are required to notify DCHA in writing thirty (30) daysrior to vacating the unit.  Failure to provide a thirty (30) day writtenotice will permit DCHA to charge the tenant with an additional month’s rentnd may result in forfeiture of all or part of the security deposit.

9811.3 9811.3 The DCHA shall conduct a move-out inspection with the AYBL Familyrior to the scheduled move-out date. If the AYBL Family vacates the unitithout notifying DCHA, DCHA shall inspect the unit within twenty-four (24)ours of becoming aware of the family vacating the unit.

9811.4 9811.4 The security deposit will be treated in accordance with the DCHAwelling Lease Agreement. Except that, in the event the tenant caused damagesxceed the amount of the security deposit, DCHA may deduct the balance due fromhe savings escrow or take any other action allowable by D.C. law.

9811.5 9811.5 DCHA shall furnish the vacated AYBL Family with a statement of totalharges and any deductions from the security deposit and/or escrow accounts forny damages within thirty (30) calendar days after completion of repairs.

Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 2460, 2469 (March 18,011); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4346, 4347May 20, 2011).
