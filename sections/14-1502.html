§ 14-1502. AUTHORITY TO CORRECT (§ 14-1502)

1502.1. The Director may consider the following factors in determining whether the Department should cause correction of a violation:

(a) The status of any current assessments against the property;

(b) The financial status of the property including whether the property is in either foreclosure or receivership, or has been abandoned;

(c) The physical condition of the property including whether there are any major structural problems;

(d) The current vacancy rate of the property, if a housing accommodation;

(e) The amount of funds needed to make the correction in relation to market value of the property;

(f) The prospect of timely repayment of the funds expended including whether there is an active Tenant’s Association paying funds into an escrow account;

(g) The best interests of the tenants in being provided safe and sanitary conditions; and

(h) Any other factors which the Director deems relevant.

SOURCE
