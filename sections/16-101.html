§ 16-101. TRUTH IN LENDING[COLON] FEDERAL LAW AND REGULATIONS (§ 16-101)

101.1. Except as otherwise specifically provided in this chapter, the following sections of the federal &#8220;Truth in Lending Act&#8221; (Pub. L. 90-321, 82 Stat. 146), as amended, are incorporated in this chapter by reference:

(a) Section 106 &#8211; Determination of finance charge;

(b) Section 107 &#8211; Determination of annual percentage rate;

(c) Section 121 &#8211; General requirements of disclosure;

(d) Section 122 &#8211; Form of disclosure;

(e) Section 124 &#8211; Effect of subsequent occurrence;

(f) Section 127 &#8211; Open end consumer credit plans;

(g) Section 128 &#8211; Sales not under open end credit plans;

(h) Section 141 &#8211; Catalogs and multiple page advertisements;

(i) Section 142 &#8211; Advertising of down payments and installments;

(j) Section 143 &#8211; Advertising of open end credit plans;

(k) Section 144 &#8211; Advertising of credit other than open end plans; and

(l) Section 145 &#8211; Non-liability of media.

101.2. Failure of any person covered by this chapter to comply with the sections of the &#8220;Truth in Lending Act&#8221; incorporated by reference in this section, as implemented by regulations issued by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, shall constitute a violation of this chapter.

101.3. Except to the extent provided in this chapter, compliance with the incorporated sections of the &#8220;Truth in Lending Act&#8221; shall constitute compliance with this section.
