§ 27-824. CONDUCT OF HEARINGS (§ 27-824)

824.1 824.1 Commission hearings shall be open to the public.

824.2 824.2 The Commission may exclude or order the removal of any participant in aearing who becomes disruptive to the hearing process.

824.3 824.3 The Commission may bar a participant who has been excluded or removedrom a hearing from participation in future hearings.

824.4 824.4 The Commission may continue a hearing if an applicant or a respondent,r a representative thereof, has been excluded or removed from the hearing inrder to provide the respondent with an opportunity to obtain anotherepresentative, unless the applicant or respondent expressly waives aontinuance.

824.5 824.5 Upon the request of either party, the Commission shall sequesteritnesses who may testify at the hearing.
