§ 17-6210. LICENSURE BY ENDORSEMENT (§ 17-6210)

6210.1. The Board may issue a license to an applicant who:

(a) Is currently licensed and in good standing under the laws of another state with standards which are substantially equivalent to the requirements of the District of Columbia laws and regulations;

(b) Has at least three (3) years of current practice as a nursing home administrator;

(c) Has passed the District examination; and

(d) Has forwarded to the Board at least (3) letters of recommendation by persons who can attest to the applicant&#8217;s qualifications as they pertain to the practice of nursing home administration.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 10629 (November 19, 2004).
