§ 9-523. MISCELLANEOUS (§ 9-523)

523.1. A transfer of a controlling interest from one (1) entity to another in which there is no change in ownership interest shall not be subject to the Recordation of Economic Interests Act.

523.2. New stock certificates issued to owners of record of a corporation based solely on a change in the name of the corporation shall not be subject to the recordation tax.

523.3. The transfer of a controlling interest by a corporation to its wholly-owned subsidiary, from a wholly-owned subsidiary to its parent, or from one wholly- owned subsidiary to another shall be considered a change of identity and not subject to the Recordation of Economic Interests Act.

523.4. The exclusion of a transfer from recordation under the Recordation of Economic Interests Act shall not prevent the same transfer from being subject to taxation under any other Act or rule of law.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 8653, 8661 (December 29, 1989).
