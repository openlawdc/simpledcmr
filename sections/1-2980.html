§ 1-2980. UNEMPLOYMENT INSURANCE CASES – SCOPE (§ 1-2980)

2980.1. Sections 2980 through 2986 contain the Rules for OAH hearings of appeals of decisions of the District of Columbia Department of Employment Services (DOES) concerning unemployment compensation insurance.

2980.2 2980.2 If Sections 2980 through 2986 do not address a procedural issue, the Rules in Chapter 28 apply.

2980.3 2980.3 If there is a conflict between any federal law or regulation and anything in these Rules, the federal law or regulation shall control.

2980.4 2980.4 If there is a conflict between any District of Columbia statute and anything in these Rules, the District of Columbia statute shall control.

2980.5 2980.5 If there is a conflict between any other agency’s procedural rules or regulations and these Rules, these Rules shall control.

Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 12541, 12591 (December 31, 2010).
