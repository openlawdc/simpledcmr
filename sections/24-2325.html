§ 24-2325. PRE-1985 PISTOLS (§ 24-2325)

2325.1 2325.1 Any pistol with a single action firing mechanism manufactured prior to985 shall be exempt from the application of § 504 of the Act (D.C. Officialode § 7-2505.04 (2012 Repl.)).

2325.2 2325.2 Any pistol manufactured prior to 1985, not subject to § 2325.1, shalle deemed included on the District Roster established pursuant to § 2323.
