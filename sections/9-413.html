§ 9-413. AMOUNT OF SALES TAX COLLECTION BY VENDORS (§ 9-413)

413.1. If the gross receipts from any sale are exempt from the tax, the vendor shall not collect reimbursement for any tax from the purchaser.

413.2. If a sale is subject to the tax, the vendor shall add to the sales price and collect reimbursement for the tax from the purchaser in the amounts set forth in §§ 413.3 through 413.8.

413.3. For each sale or charge subject to the sales tax at the two percent (2%) rate, a vendor shall collect the following reimbursement:
