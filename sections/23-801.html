§ 23-801. PRIMARY TIER VIOLATIONS (§ 23-801)

801.1 801.1 The Board may fine a licensee for a primary tier violation at a showause hearing scheduled pursuant to the notice requirements set forth in § 1604s follows:

(a) For the first primary tier violation, the fine shall be $ 1000-$ 2000.

(b) For the second primary tier violation within two years, the fine shall be $,000-$ 4,000.

(c) For the third primary tier violation in three years, the fine shall be $,000-$ 6,000.

(d) For the fourth primary tier violation in four years, the ABC license shalle revoked or fined no less than $30,000 and suspended for 30 consecutive days.
