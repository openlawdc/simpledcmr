§ 27-817. GROUNDS FOR REVOCATION (§ 27-817)

817.1 817.1 Upon a finding that the CBE is engaged in conduct set forth in section363 of the Act, the Commission may revoke the certificate of registration forhe CBE under procedures set forth in section 2363 of the Act.

817.2 817.2 In considering whether the registration of certification of a CBE shoulde revoked, in addition to any other consideration, the Commission shallonsider whether any member of the CBE has been convicted of a crime that bearsirectly on the fitness of the CBE to participate in programs establishedursuant to the Act and these regulations.

817.3 817.3 Upon revocation, the Department shall not accept, review, or evaluateor a period of twelve (12) months from the date the applicant receives noticef the decision to revoke:

(a) Amendments to or new information on the revoked certification; or

(b) A new certification application from the applicant, its agent(s),epresentative(s), or other members of the public on the applicant&#8217;sehalf.
