§ 3-101. OFFICE OF CAMPAIGN FINANCE (§ 3-101)

101.1. The Office of the Director of Campaign Finance is established by law under the jurisdiction of the District of Columbia Board of Elections and Ethics.

101.2. The Administrator of the Office of Campaign Finance is the &#8220;Director of Campaign Finance,&#8221; who is appointed by, and serves at the pleasure of, the Board.

101.3. The Director of Campaign Finance is responsible for the administrative operations of the Board pertaining to the Campaign Finance Act and other duties delegated or assigned by the Board.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 26 D.C. Reg. 2134 (November 9, 1979); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 28 D.C. Reg. 1726 (April 17, 1981), incorporating by reference the text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 28 D.C. Reg.684 (February 13, 1981); and as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 D.C. Reg. 3937 (May 15, 2009).

EDITOR’S NOTE

Subsections 100.5, 100.6 and 100.7 of the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking which was adopted by the Board as final regulations were renumbered 101.1, 101.2 and 101.3 in this section.
