§ 28-706. EFFECT OF DISCIPLINARY ACTION[SLASH]DISMISSAL (§ 28-706)

706.1. Administrative action by the Disciplinary Team, or other authority, does not constitute additional punishment and should not be construed as such.

706.2. If a guilty finding for an offense affects the defendant&#8217;s security level, the Disciplinary Team may refer the defendant to his/her Case Manager for proper reclassification.

706.3. Dismissal or modification of a sanction does not deprive the defendant&#8217;s Case Manager of the opportunity to consider the defendant&#8217;s conduct for purposes other than discipline including, but not limited to, security classification decisions.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 4558, 4568 (May 17, 2002).
