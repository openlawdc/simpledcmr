§ 21-1100. PURPOSE AND SCOPE (§ 21-1100)

1100.1. This chapter establishes the Water Quality Standards (WQS) for the waters of the District of Columbia, as authorized by section 5 of the Water Pollution Control Act of 1984, effective March 16, 1985 (D.C. Law 5188; D.C. Official Code § 8-103.04).

AUTHORITY Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is § 5 of the Water Pollution Control Act of 1984, D.C. Law 5-188, D.C. Code § 6-924 (1988 Repl. Vol.):

Mayor&#8217;s Order 85-152, dated September 12, 1985.

SOURCE
