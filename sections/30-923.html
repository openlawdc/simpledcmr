§ 30-923. EXTRA PRIZES AND PRIZE STRUCTURE (§ 30-923)

923.1. Winning EXTRA wagers may pay prizes of $ 3, $ 5, $ 10, $ 20, $ 50, $ 100, or $ 500 based on the prizes printed on a winning EXTRA ticket.

923.2. The EXTRA prize structure is based on a pool of 120,000 wagers and includes the approximate number and level of prizes per pool, odds of winning, and percentage of prizes available in each pool. The Executive Director may adopt other prize structures which shall take effect upon the issuance of instructions to the public.
