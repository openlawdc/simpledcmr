§ 19-299. DEFINITIONS (§ 19-299)

299.1 299.1 When used in this chapter, the following words and terms shall have theeanings ascribed:

Authority &#8211; the Washington Convention and Sports Authority established byhe Washington Convention Center Authority Act of 1994, effective September 28,994, D.C. Law 10-188, as amended by the Fiscal Year 2010 Budget Support Act of009, effective March 3, 2010 (D.C. Law 18-111; D.C. Official Code §§0-1201.01, et seq. (2008 Repl. &#038; 2011 Supp.))

Board &#8211; the Board of Directors of the Authority.

Casual employee &#8211; an employee whose terms of appointment provide formployment either: (1) for a brief period of time or (2) on a casual or aseeded basis.

Executive Staff &#8211; an employee at the level of Division Director or above.

President and Chief Executive Officer- the President and Chief Executive Officerf the Authority or his or her designee.

Public body &#8211; any body described by § 1503 of the District of Columbiaovernment Comprehensive Merit Personnel Act of 1979, D.C. Official Code §-616.3 (2006 Repl..).

Regular full-time employee &#8211; an employee whose terms of appointmentrovide that the employee shall work at least forty (40) hours per week either:1) for an unlimited or extended period of time or; (2) for as long as funds arevailable and the position is necessary to the objectives of the Authority.

Regular part-time employee &#8211; an employee whose terms of appointmentrovide that the employee shall work less than forty (40) hours per Week either:1) for an unlimited or extended period of time or; (2) for as long as funds arevailable and the position is necessary to the objectives of the Authority.

Relative &#8211; the spouse, parent, parent-in-law, child, step-child, sister,rother, sister-in-law, brother-in-law, step-parent, aunt, uncle, niece, nephew,randparent, grandchild or registered domestic partner (as defined by Chapter 7f Title 32 of the District of Columbia Official Code) of an employee of theuthority.

Vice President &#8211; the Vice President, Human Resources appointed by theresident and Chief Executive Officer of the Authority.
