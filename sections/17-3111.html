§ 17-3111. VARIANCES (§ 17-3111)

3111.1 3111.1 The Director may excuse the inability of a licensee to conform toequirements of section 3116 of this chapter and grant a variance to theicensee if the Director determines the following:

(a) To deny the variances would result in undue hardship to the licensee;

(b) Compensating factors are present which give adequate protection to theealth, safety, and welfare of the public; and

(c) The variance can be granted without impairing the purposes of this chapterr the act.

3111.2 3111.2 To apply for a variance a licensee shall state on a DCRA form theeasons a variance should be granted based upon factors listed in section111.1.

3113.3 3113.3 A licensee shall submit a request for a variance within the periodpecified in the notice of noncompliance, but in no more than twenty (20) daysfter the date the notice is mailed to the licensee.

3113.4 3113.4 The Director shall maintain a written record of each variance granted orenied and shall make the record available for public inspection.

3113.5 3113.5 The Director’s decision on a variance is final and is not subject todministrative review.

Source: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7853, 7860 (October 23, 1992).
