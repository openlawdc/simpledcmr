§ 17-3120. GROUNDS FOR DENIAL, REVOCATION, OROTHER DISCIPLINARY ACTION (§ 17-3120)

3120.1 3120.1 Upon finding that an applicant for or a holder of a license engaged inonduct described in section 3120.2, the Director may, after providing noticend an opportunity for a hearing pursuant to this chapter, take one or more ofhe following actions:

(a) Deny a license;

(b) Suspend or revoke a license;

(c) Refuse to renew or reinstate a license;

(d) Impose a civil fine pursuant to procedures established by the Department ofonsumer and Regulatory Affairs Civil Infractions Act of 1985, D.C. Code,ection 6-2701 et seq.; or

(e) Impose restrictions on the license to operate a funeral servicesstablishment.

3120.2 3120.2 The Director may take one (1) or more of the actions listed in section120.1 if the Director finds that an applicant for a license or a licenseeommits one of the following acts:

(a) Engages in any conduct prohibited by D.C. Code, section 2-208;

(b) Violates any provision of the act, this chapter, Chapter 30 of this title,r other District or federal law pertaining to the practice of funeral directingr funeral service establishment;

(c) Denies access by the Director to a funeral services establishment or itsecords;

(d) Fails to comply with an order of or agreement with the Director; or

(e) Fails to pay a civil fine imposed pursuant to the Department of Consumernd Regulatory Affairs Civil Infractions Act of 1985, D.C. Law 6-42, D.C. Code,ection 6-2701 et seq.

Source: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7853, 7863 (October 23, 1992).
