§ 7-1514. ORDER OF BUSINESS FOR REGULAR MEETINGS (§ 7-1514)

1514.1. The Board shall be called to order at the time and place designated pursuant to § 1509.

1514.2. The Chairman shall ascertain the presence of a quorum before any business is transacted.

1514.3. The Board shall take up business, after a quorum is ascertained, in the following order:

(a) Approval of the minutes of the previous meeting;

(b) Approval of the Consent Agenda without objection by another member;

(c) Chairman&#8217;s Comments;

(d) Report of the Executive Director;

(e) Reports of Standing Committees;

(f) Reports of Special Committees;

(g) Final vote on rules that have been pending at least thirty (30) days since the publication of Notice of Proposed, Final, and/or Emergency Rulemaking in the District of Columbia Register pursuant to D.C. Code §2-205(a) (2001);

(h) Official communications received from other governmental agencies;

(i) Other business previously placed on the agenda; and

(j) Other business not previously placed on the agenda.

1514.4. Items listed in §1514.3 which require Board action or approval cannot be acted upon in the absence of a quorum. The Board may, at the beginning of a meeting, set a different order for a particular meeting, in which case the order set by the Board shall be followed for that meeting, subject to the provisions of §1516.

SOURCE

Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10803, 10804 (November 29, 2002).
