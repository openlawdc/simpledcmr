§ 1-108. DISHONORED CHECKS (§ 1-108)

108.1. A fifty dollars ($50.00) fee shall be imposed on any person who gives or causes to be given in payment of any tax, assessment, fee, charge, or other obligation due the District of Columbia, a check which is dishonored or not duly paid.

AUTHORITY

The authority for this section is the Dishonored Check Collection Fee Act of 1981, as amended, D.C. Law 4-16 (codified at D.C. Code § 1-357 (1999 Repl.).

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 7204 (November 29, 1991).
