The District of Columbia Public Library’s behavior rules have a threefoldurpose: to protect the rights and safety of library customers, to protect theights and safety of staff members, and to preserve the library’s materials,acilities, and property.

The DC Public Library supports the rights of all individuals to free and equalccess to information and use of the library without discrimination,ntimidation, threat of harm or invasion of privacy. The DC Public Library isedicated to providing friendly, courteous and respectful service, and annjoyable, clean, and comfortable environment for all Library users.
