§ 7-134. PAYMENT OF COMPENSATION BENEFITS ON REMAND FROM APPEAL (§ 7-134)

134.1 134.1 If an ALJ orders the Program to pay compensation to a claimant pursuanto section 2324 of the Act, the Program shall pay compensation to the claimantithin thirty (30) days of the date of the order, provided that the Programhall require a claimant to provide within fifteen (15) days of the order, prioro the payment, the following:

Verification of the injury for the period specified in the Order; and

Verification of lost wages for the period specified in the Order, including butot limited to all wage documentation for the period (such as, pay stubs, W-2,r 1099 income tax forms, and/or other related income earnings statements), andny other earnings information as authorized by § 104 of this chapter.

SOURCE

Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8766, 8805 (July 27, 2012).
