§ 17-2408. CERTIFICATION OF RECORDS (§ 17-2408)

2408.1. The Mayor shall issue certifications of the signatures of District of Columbia governmental officials who are required to sign documents of public records.

2408.2. The fee charged for each certification issued under this section shall be $15.00.

SOURCE

§ 1.8(a) of Regulation No. 73-13, approved May 24, 1973, 19 DCR 1147 (June 11, 1973), as added by § 302 of District of Columbia Revenue Act of 1983, D.C. Law 5-14; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 43 DCR 3365 (June 30, 1995); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 5052 (June 11, 2010).
