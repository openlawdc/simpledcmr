§ 11-632. GROSS FLOOR AREA (CR) (§ 11-632)

632.1 632.1 A public recreation and community center shall not exceed a gross floorrea of forty thousand square feet (40,000 sq. ft.), unless approved by theoard of Zoning Adjustment as a special exception in accordance with therovisions of § 3104.1.

SOURCE

31 DCR 6585, 6586 (December 28, 1984); as amended by Final Rulemakingublished at 50 DCR 10137 (November 28, 2003).
