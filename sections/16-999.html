§ 16-999. DEFINITIONS (§ 16-999)

999.1. The words defined in § 1 of the Act (D.C. Official Code § 47-2884.01) shall have the same meaning when used in this chapter.

999.2. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have the meanings ascribed:

Pledge &#8211; an article or articles or personal property of other valuable thing deposited with a pawnbroker as security for a loan.

Pledgor &#8211; the person who delivers a pledge into the possession of a pawnbroker.

Director &#8211; the Director of the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs or the Director&#8217;s agent, representative, or designee.

Chief of Police &#8211; the Chief of Police, Metropolitan Police Department of the District of Columbia.
