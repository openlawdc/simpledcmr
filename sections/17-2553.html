§ 17-2553. ELECTRONIC SUBMISSION OF PEER REVIEW REPORTS TO BOARD (§ 17-2553)

2553.1 2553.1 In lieu of submitting a hard copy of a peer review report and allccompanying documents, a firm manager may notify the Board that the documentsequested can be viewed and downloaded at the website of the sponsoringrganization. The firm manager may also email a copy of the peer review programeport and any supporting documents to the Board.

2553.2 2553.2 If the Board is not able to download a copy of the requested documents,he firm manager shall submit the required hard copy to the Board.
