§ 23-1300. TRANSPORT PERMITS FOR ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES (§ 23-1300)

1300.1. The Board may issue a transport permit to the holder of a Retailer&#8217;s license to transport, or cause to be transported into the District of Columbia, alcoholic beverages when the Board is satisfied that beverages bearing the same brand or trade name are not obtainable by the retail licensee from a licensed manufacturer or wholesaler in the District of Columbia in sufficient quantity to reasonably satisfy the immediate needs of the retail licensee.

1300.2. A transport permit issued under § 1300.1 shall specifically set forth the quantity, character, and brand or trade name of beverages to be transported and the names and addresses of the seller and of the purchaser.

AUTHORITY
