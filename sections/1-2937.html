§ 1-2937. RENTAL HOUSING CASES – FINAL ORDERS (§ 1-2937)

2937.1 2937.1 OAH shall serve all final orders on the parties by first-class mail.

OAH also shall serve all final orders on the Rent Administrator and theommission.
